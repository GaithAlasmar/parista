# Mint Mocha

A refreshing twist on the classic mocha, featuring cool mint and rich chocolate.

---

## Ingredients
- 1 shot (30ml) of Gia or Miscela Bar espresso
- 15ml Monin Dark Chocolate Sauce
- 10ml Monin Frosted Mint Syrup
- 200ml steamed milk
- Crushed mint candies or chocolate shavings for garnish

---

## Instructions
1. Brew a single shot of Gia or Miscela Bar espresso into a serving cup.
2. Add both the Monin Dark Chocolate Sauce and Monin Frosted Mint Syrup to the espresso.
3. Stir well until all the syrups and sauce are completely dissolved.
4. Steam the milk until it has a silky and smooth microfoam.
5. Pour the milk steadily into the espresso base.
6. Garnish with chocolate shavings or crushed mint candies.

---

## Pro-Tips
- Adjust the mint syrup carefully; mint can easily overpower the coffee and chocolate flavors if too much is added.
