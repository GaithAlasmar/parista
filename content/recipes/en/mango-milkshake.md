# Mango Milkshake
A tropical, refreshing, and creamy mango milkshake.

## Ingredients
- 150 ml cold whole milk
- 3 scoops (150 g) premium vanilla ice cream
- 30 ml Monin Mango puree
- 50 g fresh or frozen mango chunks
- Whipped cream (for garnish)
- A drizzle of Monin Mango puree (for garnish)

## Instructions
1. Place the cold whole milk, vanilla ice cream, fresh/frozen mango chunks, and Monin Mango puree into the Sonifer commercial blender.
2. Blend on high speed for about 40 seconds until the fruit is completely pureed and the milkshake is smooth.
3. Pour the thick mango milkshake into a 400 ml serving glass.
4. Top with whipped cream.
5. Finish by drizzling a little extra Monin Mango puree over the whipped cream.

## Pro-Tips
- Frozen mango chunks will make the shake thicker without the need for additional ice.
- A dash of cardamom can add an exotic twist to this tropical drink.
