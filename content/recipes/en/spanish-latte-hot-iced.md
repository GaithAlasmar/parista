# Spanish Latte (Hot & Iced)

A highly requested cafe staple. The Spanish Latte balances the robust flavour of Miscela Bar or Gia espresso with the creamy sweetness of Monin White Mocha Sauce. Served either hot or iced.

---

## Ingredients

**For Iced:**
- **20 ml** Monin White Chocolate (White Mocha) Sauce
- **10 ml** Monin Vanilla Syrup (optional)
- **150 ml** cold whole milk
- **30 ml** double espresso — **Gia beans** or **Miscela Bar**
- **200 g** ice

**For Hot:**
- **20 ml** Monin White Chocolate (White Mocha) Sauce
- **10 ml** Monin Vanilla Syrup (optional)
- **180 ml** whole milk, steamed
- **30 ml** double espresso

---

## Instructions

**Iced Spanish Latte:**
1. **Prepare the glass:** Add 20 ml of Monin White Mocha Sauce and 10 ml of Vanilla Syrup to the serving glass.
2. **Add ice and milk:** Fill the glass with ice cubes, then pour in 150 ml of cold milk. Stir slightly to mix the milk and sauce at the bottom.
3. **Pull espresso:** Extract a double shot of espresso.
4. **Float:** Slowly pour the hot espresso over the ice to create a beautiful gradient layer.

**Hot Spanish Latte:**
1. **Prepare the cup:** Add the White Mocha Sauce and Vanilla Syrup into a pre-warmed mug.
2. **Pull espresso:** Extract the double shot directly into the mug and stir to melt the sauce.
3. **Steam milk:** Steam 180 ml of milk to 60-65°C with silky microfoam.
4. **Pour:** Pour the steamed milk over the base to integrate, finishing with latte art.

---

## Pro-Tips

- **Layering the Iced Spanish Latte:** Pouring the espresso slowly over a single piece of ice at the top ensures a perfect, distinct coffee layer that looks great when served.
- **Sweetness adjustment:** The traditional recipe is quite sweet. Adjust the White Mocha Sauce down to 15 ml for a more coffee-forward taste.
- **Bean choice:** Miscela Bar beans offer a milder profile that blends smoothly with the creamy sweetness, while Gia beans cut through for a bolder coffee punch.
