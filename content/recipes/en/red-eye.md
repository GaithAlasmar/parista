# Red Eye
A regular drip coffee with a single espresso shot added to kickstart your day.

---
## Ingredients
- 200 ml freshly brewed drip coffee
- 1 shot (30 ml) "Gia" or "Miscela Bar" espresso

---
## Instructions
1. Brew a fresh cup of drip coffee.
2. Extract a single shot of "Gia" or "Miscela Bar" espresso into a shot glass.
3. Pour the espresso shot directly into the brewed coffee.
4. Serve immediately.

---
## Pro-Tips
- Match the roast profile of your drip coffee and espresso for a harmonious taste.
