# Watermelon Mojito
A sweet and hydrating summer favorite that blends fresh watermelon flavor with cooling mint.

---
## Ingredients
- 20 ml Monin Watermelon Syrup
- 10 ml Monin Mojito Mint Syrup
- 8 fresh mint leaves
- 2 lime wedges
- 200 ml sparkling water
- 150 g ice cubes
- Small watermelon cubes for garnish

---
## Instructions
1. Gently muddle the mint leaves and lime wedges in the bottom of a glass.
2. Add the Monin Watermelon Syrup and Monin Mojito Mint Syrup.
3. Fill the glass with ice cubes.
4. Top up with sparkling water.
5. Stir well from the bottom to ensure the syrups are fully mixed.
6. Garnish with a few small watermelon cubes and a mint sprig.

---
## Pro-Tips
- Crushed ice can be used instead of cubes for a more slushy, refreshing texture.
- If available, adding a small splash of fresh watermelon juice enhances the natural flavor.
