# Vienna Coffee
A traditional Austrian coffee made with a strong double espresso topped with rich whipped cream.

---
## Ingredients
- 2 shots (60 ml) "Gia" or "Miscela Bar" espresso
- 60 ml hot water (to slightly dilute the espresso)
- 30 g heavily whipped cream
- 10 ml Monin Chocolate sauce (optional garnish)

---
## Instructions
1. Extract a double shot of "Gia" or "Miscela Bar" espresso into a warmed cup.
2. Add the hot water to lengthen the espresso slightly.
3. Spoon the freshly whipped cream generously over the top of the coffee.
4. Drizzle with Monin Chocolate sauce or cocoa powder if desired.
5. Serve without stirring, as it is meant to be sipped through the cream.

---
## Pro-Tips
- Ensure the whipped cream is thick but still soft enough to rest delicately on top of the crema without sinking.
