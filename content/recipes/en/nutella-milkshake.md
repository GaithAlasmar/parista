# Nutella Milkshake
A rich and indulgent milkshake loaded with Nutella.

## Ingredients
- 150 ml cold whole milk
- 3 scoops (150 g) premium vanilla or chocolate ice cream
- 60 g Nutella
- 10 ml Monin Hazelnut syrup
- 10 ml Monin Dark Chocolate sauce (for drizzling)
- Whipped cream (for garnish)
- Chocolate shavings (for garnish)

## Instructions
1. Drizzle the Monin Dark Chocolate sauce and a little bit of Nutella around the inside of a 400 ml glass.
2. Add the cold whole milk, ice cream, Nutella, and Monin Hazelnut syrup into the Sonifer commercial blender.
3. Blend on high speed for 30-40 seconds until all ingredients are well combined and smooth.
4. Pour the rich milkshake into the prepared glass.
5. Top with a mountain of whipped cream and sprinkle chocolate shavings.

## Pro-Tips
- Warm the Nutella slightly before adding it to the blender so it mixes more evenly.
- Chill the serving glass beforehand to keep the milkshake colder for longer.
