# Pistachio Latte

The star of modern café menus. A **double espresso and steamed milk base enriched with 20g of Pistachio Sauce** — producing a subtly sweet, richly nutty, and beautifully pale green latte that photographs as beautifully as it tastes.

---

## Ingredients

- **30ml** single espresso (9g) — **Gia beans** or **Miscela Bar**
- **20g** Pistachio Sauce (Monin or equivalent)
- **180ml** whole milk, steamed to silky microfoam
- Optional: crushed pistachios or pistachio dust for garnish

---

## Instructions

1. **Pistachio base.** Spoon **20g of Pistachio Sauce** into the bottom of a pre-warmed cup. The sauce is thick — swirl the cup to spread it.
2. **Pull the espresso.** Extract **9g** and pour directly over the sauce. Stir vigorously — pistachio sauce needs more mixing than syrup to fully incorporate with the espresso.
3. **Steam the milk.** Steam **220ml** of whole milk to **60–65°C** with smooth, velvety microfoam.
4. **Pour.** Pour steamed milk into the pistachio-espresso base. The pistachio will give the drink a subtle greenish-beige tint.
5. **Garnish.** Optionally, sprinkle a small amount of crushed pistachios on the foam for a premium finishing touch.
6. Serve immediately.

---

## Pro-Tips

- **Pistachio sauce is thicker than syrup** — ensure it's fully incorporated before adding milk or it will clump at the bottom.
- For an **Iced Pistachio Latte:** sauce → ice → cold milk → espresso float → pistachio garnish.
- The **mild earthiness** of Miscela Bar beans complements pistachio's delicate flavour better than the bold Gia beans.
- A light dusting of **ground cardamom** on top creates a Middle Eastern-inspired flavour pairing with pistachio.
