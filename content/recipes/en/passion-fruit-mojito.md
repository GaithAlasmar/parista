# Passion Fruit Mojito

A tropical twist on the classic mocktail. Monin Passion Fruit Syrup provides a sweet-tart, exotic profile that pairs flawlessly with fresh mint and lime.

---

## Ingredients

- **20 ml** Monin Passion Fruit Syrup
- **1/2** fresh lime, cut into wedges
- **8-10** fresh mint leaves
- **150 ml** soda water (club soda or Sprite/7Up)
- **200 g** crushed ice
- Fresh mint sprig and lime wheel for garnish
- *Optional:* 1 scoop of fresh passion fruit pulp

---

## Instructions

1. **Muddle:** Drop the lime wedges and mint leaves into a highball glass. Muddle gently to extract juices and oils without tearing the mint.
2. **Add syrup:** Pour in 20 ml of Monin Passion Fruit Syrup. (Add fresh passion fruit pulp here if available).
3. **Ice:** Pack the glass completely full with crushed ice.
4. **Top and mix:** Pour soda water over the ice. Use a long bar spoon to pull the syrup and mint from the bottom up through the ice.
5. **Garnish:** Top with a sprig of fresh mint and a lime wheel.

---

## Pro-Tips

- **Visual appeal:** Adding just a teaspoon of real passion fruit seeds/pulp elevates this drink from standard to premium instantly.
- **The "slap":** Before garnishing with the top mint sprig, clap it once sharply between your hands. This releases the aromatic oils so the customer smells fresh mint on every sip.
- **Proper mixing:** If not stirred properly, all the heavy syrup will remain at the bottom. Ensure the bar spoon reaches the very bottom of the glass.
