# Pineapple Smoothie
A sweet, tropical pineapple smoothie that tastes like a beach vacation.

---
## Ingredients
- 150g fresh or frozen pineapple chunks
- 30ml Monin Pineapple Puree or Coconut Syrup
- 100ml coconut milk or water
- 150g ice cubes

---
## Instructions
1. Place the pineapple chunks, Monin Pineapple Puree, coconut milk, and ice cubes into the Sonifer commercial blender.
2. Blend on high speed for 40 seconds until the mixture is frothy and smooth.
3. Pour into a tall glass and serve.

---
## Pro-Tips
- Use frozen pineapple chunks for a frostier, thicker texture.
- A splash of lime juice elevates the tropical flavor profile.
