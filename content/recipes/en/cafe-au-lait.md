# Café au Lait
A traditional French drink made of equal parts brewed coffee and steamed milk.

---
## Ingredients
- 125 ml strong brewed coffee (or 2 shots/60 ml of "Gia" or "Miscela Bar" espresso diluted with 65 ml hot water)
- 125 ml steamed milk

---
## Instructions
1. Brew a strong cup of coffee or prepare diluted "Gia" or "Miscela Bar" espresso shots.
2. Steam the milk until it is hot but has very little foam.
3. Pour the coffee and steamed milk simultaneously into a wide cup.
4. Serve warm.

---
## Pro-Tips
- In France, this is typically served in a bowl-like cup to allow for dipping pastries. Keep the milk foam to a minimum.
