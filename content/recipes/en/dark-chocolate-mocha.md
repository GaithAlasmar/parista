# Dark Chocolate Mocha

A rich and indulgent blend of espresso, dark chocolate, and velvety milk.

---

## Ingredients
- 1 shot (30ml) of Gia or Miscela Bar espresso
- 20ml Monin Dark Chocolate Sauce
- 200ml steamed milk
- Whipped cream (optional)
- Cocoa powder for garnish

---

## Instructions
1. Extract a single shot of Gia or Miscela Bar espresso into your cup.
2. Add the Monin Dark Chocolate Sauce to the hot espresso.
3. Stir vigorously until the sauce and espresso are completely combined into a smooth paste.
4. Steam the milk to latte temperature (60-65°C) with a silky microfoam.
5. Pour the steamed milk over the chocolate-espresso mixture.
6. Top with whipped cream and a dusting of cocoa powder if desired.

---

## Pro-Tips
- Dark chocolate sauce is thick; ensure you stir it immediately with the hot espresso so it doesn't clump at the bottom.
