# Iced Mocha

The classic coffee-chocolate combination: **Gia espresso**, cold milk, and **Monin Dark Chocolate Sauce** — poured over ice for a refreshing, rich, and slightly bitter-sweet drink. Simple to build, consistently delicious.

---

## Ingredients

- 250 ml ice
- 150 ml whole milk
- 2 shots (60 ml) double espresso — **Gia beans**
- 25 ml **Monin Dark Chocolate Sauce**

---

## Method

1. **Pull the espresso.** Extract a double shot from freshly ground **Gia beans**. Set aside.
2. **Chocolate base.** Add **25 ml Monin Dark Chocolate Sauce** to the bottom of the cup and swirl to coat.
3. **Fill with ice.** Pack the cup with ice cubes.
4. **Pour the milk.** Add **150 ml whole milk** over the ice — it blends with the chocolate on the way down.
5. **Add the espresso.** Pour the double shot directly over the milk — no need to float; the mocha is a blended drink.
6. **Stir once** from bottom to top with a long spoon to incorporate the chocolate.
7. Serve immediately with a straw.

---

## Tips

- For a **richer mocha**: increase chocolate sauce to 30 ml or add a squeeze of **Monin Caramel Sauce** alongside the chocolate.
- For a **hot mocha**: skip ice, add steamed milk (60–65 °C), and stir the chocolate sauce through before topping with a thin layer of foam.
- A sprinkle of **cocoa powder** on top adds a professional finish.
- **Miscela Bar** beans work exceptionally well here — their mild, balanced flavour lets the chocolate shine.
