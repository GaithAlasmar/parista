# Dead Eye
A regular drip coffee with a triple espresso shot added. Not for the faint of heart.

---
## Ingredients
- 200 ml freshly brewed drip coffee
- 3 shots (90 ml) "Gia" or "Miscela Bar" espresso

---
## Instructions
1. Prepare the drip coffee and pour into a large mug.
2. Extract three shots of "Gia" or "Miscela Bar" espresso.
3. Pour the espresso shots into the coffee.
4. Serve immediately.

---
## Pro-Tips
- To balance the intense acidity and bitterness, consider a dark roast for the drip coffee to add body.
