# Mango Smoothie
A tropical, refreshing mango smoothie blended to perfection.

---
## Ingredients
- 150g fresh or frozen mango chunks
- 30ml Monin Mango Puree
- 150ml milk or yogurt
- 100g ice cubes

---
## Instructions
1. Add the mango chunks, Monin Mango Puree, milk (or yogurt), and ice cubes into the Sonifer commercial blender.
2. Blend on high speed for 30-45 seconds until smooth and creamy.
3. Pour into a chilled glass and serve immediately.

---
## Pro-Tips
- Garnish with a fresh mint leaf or a slice of mango for a premium look.
- Use frozen mango for a thicker consistency.
