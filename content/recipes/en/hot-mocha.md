# Hot Mocha

The classic blend of rich espresso, steamed milk, and dark chocolate. This "Best Seller" uses Monin Dark Chocolate Sauce and Gia espresso for a deeply comforting and balanced hot drink.

---

## Ingredients

- **25 ml** Monin Dark Chocolate Sauce
- **30 ml** double espresso (18g in, 30-36g out) — **Gia beans**
- **180 ml** whole milk, steamed to microfoam

---

## Instructions

1. **Prepare the base:** Squeeze 25 ml of Monin Dark Chocolate Sauce into the bottom of a pre-warmed cup.
2. **Pull the espresso:** Extract a double shot of Gia espresso directly over the chocolate sauce.
3. **Mix:** Stir the espresso and chocolate sauce vigorously until fully combined and smooth.
4. **Steam the milk:** Steam 180 ml of whole milk to 60-65°C, creating a smooth and velvety microfoam.
5. **Pour:** Slowly pour the steamed milk over the chocolate-espresso base, integrating the liquids. Create latte art if desired.

---

## Pro-Tips

- **Ensure full mixing:** Dark chocolate sauce is thick. If not stirred properly with the hot espresso, it will clump at the bottom of the cup.
- **Milk texture:** Aim for latte-style microfoam rather than stiff cappuccino foam to keep the drink silky.
- **Garnish:** Lightly dust the top with cocoa powder for an elevated presentation.
