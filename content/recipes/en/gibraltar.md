# Gibraltar
A perfectly balanced short drink served in a specific faceted glass, featuring equal parts espresso and lightly steamed milk.

---
## Ingredients
- 2 shots (60 ml) "Gia" or "Miscela Bar" espresso
- 60 ml steamed milk

---
## Instructions
1. Extract a double shot of "Gia" or "Miscela Bar" espresso into a 4.5 oz Gibraltar glass.
2. Steam the milk gently to achieve a slightly cooler temperature (around 55°C) and thin microfoam.
3. Pour the steamed milk over the espresso, integrating it smoothly.
4. Serve immediately to enjoy its distinct texture.

---
## Pro-Tips
- A Gibraltar is similar to a cortado but defined by its glassware and slightly cooler milk, allowing it to be consumed quickly.
