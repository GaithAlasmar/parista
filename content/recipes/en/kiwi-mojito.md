# Kiwi Mojito
A vibrant and tangy mojito bursting with the exotic and sweet flavor of kiwi.

---
## Ingredients
- 20 ml Monin Kiwi Purée (or Syrup)
- 10 ml Monin Mojito Mint Syrup
- 8 fresh mint leaves
- 2 lime wedges
- 200 ml sparkling water
- 150 g ice cubes
- Fresh kiwi slices for garnish

---
## Instructions
1. Lightly muddle the mint leaves and lime wedges in a serving glass.
2. Add the Monin Kiwi Purée and Monin Mojito Mint Syrup.
3. Fill the glass with ice cubes.
4. Top with sparkling water.
5. Stir gently to mix the purée and syrups with the sparkling water.
6. Garnish with a slice of fresh kiwi and a mint sprig.

---
## Pro-Tips
- Muddle a slice of fresh kiwi along with the mint for an extra burst of texture and flavor.
- Ensure the sparkling water is well chilled before making the drink to keep it maximally refreshing.
