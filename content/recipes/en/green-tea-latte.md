# Green Tea Latte
A smooth and creamy green tea latte made with high-quality matcha powder and perfectly steamed milk.

---
## Ingredients
- 5g Premium Matcha Powder
- 30ml Hot Water (80°C)
- 15ml Monin Vanilla Syrup (optional)
- 200ml Whole Milk

---
## Instructions
1. Sift the matcha powder into a cup to prevent clumps.
2. Add the hot water and whisk vigorously until smooth and frothy.
3. Add the Monin Vanilla Syrup if a sweeter taste is desired.
4. Steam the whole milk to 60-65°C, ensuring a smooth microfoam.
5. Pour the steamed milk over the matcha base.
6. Serve immediately.

---
## Pro-Tips
- Water that is too hot will make the matcha taste bitter; aim for 80°C.
- Use a traditional bamboo whisk (chasen) for the best texture and froth.
