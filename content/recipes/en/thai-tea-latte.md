# Thai Tea Latte
A sweet, creamy, and spiced Thai tea latte served over ice, featuring a distinct vibrant orange color.

---
## Ingredients
- 15g Thai Tea Mix
- 120ml Hot Water (95°C)
- 30ml Sweetened Condensed Milk
- 15ml Evaporated Milk
- 10ml Monin Cane Sugar Syrup (optional)
- 100ml Whole Milk
- 150g Ice Cubes

---
## Instructions
1. Steep the Thai tea mix in hot water for 5 minutes, then strain well.
2. While the tea is hot, stir in the sweetened condensed milk and Monin Cane Sugar Syrup until dissolved.
3. Fill a glass with ice cubes.
4. Pour the sweetened tea mixture over the ice.
5. Gently pour the whole milk and evaporated milk over the top to create a layered effect.
6. Stir before drinking.

---
## Pro-Tips
- Adjust the sweetness by varying the amount of condensed milk or syrup.
- For a richer texture, replace whole milk with half-and-half or a dash of cream.
