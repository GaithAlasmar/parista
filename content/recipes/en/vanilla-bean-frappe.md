# Vanilla Bean Frappe

A coffee-free blended frappe with a clean, sweet vanilla character — built on **Monin Vanilla Syrup** and frappe base, blended to a smooth thick consistency in the **Sonifer blender**. Perfect for non-coffee drinkers.

---

## Ingredients

- 250 g ice (crushed or cubed)
- 150 ml whole milk
- 30 ml frappe base powder (dissolved in 30 ml cold water)
- 30 ml **Monin Vanilla Syrup**
- Whipped cream — for topping
- **Monin Vanilla Syrup** or caramel drizzle — for topping

---

## Method

1. **Prepare frappe base.** Dissolve **30 ml frappe base powder** in **30 ml cold water** and stir until fully smooth — no lumps.
2. **Load the Sonifer blender** in this order:
   - 150 ml whole milk *(first — protects blades)*
   - 30 ml **Monin Vanilla Syrup**
   - Dissolved frappe base
   - 250 g ice *(last — on top)*
3. **Blend on high** for **35–45 seconds** until silky smooth with no ice chunks visible.
4. **Consistency check:** Should coat the back of a spoon. Too thick → add 20 ml milk and pulse 5 s. Too thin → add a handful of ice and re-blend 10 s.
2. **Frappe Base Powder (30g - 40g)** (adds texture and prevents separation)
5. **Pour into cup** — no need for a wall drizzle on vanilla, the ivory colour looks clean.
6. **Top with whipped cream** in a circular mound.
7. Finish with a drizzle of **Monin Vanilla Syrup** or **Monin Caramel Sauce** over the cream.
8. Cap with dome lid and serve immediately.

---

## Tips

- This is a **caffeine-free** drink — make sure the customer is aware if they expect coffee.
- For a **Vanilla Caramel Frappe** variant: split 15 ml vanilla + 15 ml Monin Caramel Syrup.
- For extra thickness: use slightly less milk (130 ml) and more ice.
- Always build to order.
