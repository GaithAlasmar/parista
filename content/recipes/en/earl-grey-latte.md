# Earl Grey Latte
Also known as a London Fog, this comforting drink combines strong Earl Grey tea with vanilla and steamed milk.

---
## Ingredients
- 1 Earl Grey Tea Bag (or 4g loose leaf tea)
- 120ml Hot Water (95°C)
- 15ml Monin Vanilla Syrup
- 120ml Whole Milk

---
## Instructions
1. Steep the Earl Grey tea in the hot water for 4-5 minutes to create a strong concentrate.
2. Remove the tea bag or strain the loose leaves.
3. Stir the Monin Vanilla Syrup into the hot tea.
4. Steam the whole milk to 60-65°C, creating a moderate amount of foam.
5. Pour the steamed milk over the tea mixture.
6. Serve hot, optionally garnished with a pinch of dried lavender or lemon zest.

---
## Pro-Tips
- Ensure the tea concentrate is strong enough to cut through the milk and syrup flavors.
- Steeping for longer than 5 minutes may extract bitter tannins; 4-5 minutes is optimal.
