# Vanilla Sweet Cream Cold Brew
A rich cold brew coffee topped with a velvety, house-made vanilla sweet cream cold foam.

---

## Ingredients
- 200 ml cold brew coffee
- 15 ml Monin Vanilla Syrup (for the coffee)
- 10 ml Monin Vanilla Syrup (for the sweet cream)
- 30 ml heavy cream
- 20 ml whole milk
- 150 g ice cubes

---

## Instructions
1. In a small pitcher or milk frother, combine the heavy cream, whole milk, and 10 ml of Monin Vanilla Syrup. Froth until it thickens into a soft cold foam.
2. Fill a glass with ice cubes.
3. Pour the 15 ml of Monin Vanilla Syrup and cold brew coffee into the glass and stir well.
4. Gently pour the vanilla sweet cream over the back of a spoon onto the cold brew so it floats.
5. Serve immediately with a straw.

---

## Pro-Tips
- Keep the heavy cream and milk extremely cold for the best foaming results.
- Do not over-froth the sweet cream; it should be pourable and velvety, not stiff like whipped cream.
