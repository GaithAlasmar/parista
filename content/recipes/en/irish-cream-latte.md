# Irish Cream Latte

A smooth and creamy latte flavored with the distinctive taste of non-alcoholic Irish Cream.

---

## Ingredients
- 1 shot (30 ml) Gia or Miscela Bar espresso
- 15 ml Monin Irish Syrup (non-alcoholic)
- 200 ml steamed milk
- Cocoa powder for dusting

---

## Instructions
1. Extract a single shot of Gia or Miscela Bar espresso.
2. Add the Monin Irish Syrup to the espresso in a tall glass or mug.
3. Steam your milk until perfectly textured and hot.
4. Pour the milk into the cup, integrating it with the syrup and espresso.
5. Finish with a light dusting of cocoa powder.

---

## Pro-Tips
- The complex roasted notes of Monin Irish Syrup balance perfectly with a robust espresso like Gia.
- Excellent served iced: simply build over ice and use cold milk instead.
