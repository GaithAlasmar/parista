# Lotus Milkshake
A sweet and spiced milkshake bursting with Lotus Biscoff flavor.

## Ingredients
- 150 ml cold whole milk
- 3 scoops (150 g) premium vanilla ice cream
- 40 g Lotus Biscoff spread
- 15 ml Monin Caramel syrup
- 2 Lotus Biscoff cookies (crushed)
- Whipped cream (for garnish)
- 1 whole Lotus cookie (for garnish)

## Instructions
1. Drizzle a little bit of melted Lotus Biscoff spread around the inside of a 400 ml glass.
2. In a Sonifer commercial blender, combine the cold whole milk, vanilla ice cream, Lotus Biscoff spread, crushed cookies, and Monin Caramel syrup.
3. Blend on medium-high speed for 30-40 seconds until smooth and perfectly blended.
4. Pour the milkshake into the prepared glass.
5. Top generously with whipped cream, sprinkle extra cookie crumbs if desired, and place a whole Lotus cookie on top.

## Pro-Tips
- Substitute Monin Caramel syrup with Monin Cinnamon syrup to highlight the spiced flavor of the biscuits.
- Melt the Lotus spread for a few seconds in the microwave to make it easier to blend and drizzle.
