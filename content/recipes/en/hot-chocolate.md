# Hot Chocolate

The ultimate comfort drink — **30g of dark chocolate sauce dissolved in 250ml of silky steamed milk**. No espresso, no coffee — just pure, rich chocolate in its most comforting form. Perfect for all ages, including children (with adjusted temperature).

---

## Ingredients

- **250ml** whole milk
- **30g** Monin Dark Chocolate Sauce (or equivalent)
- Optional toppings: whipped cream, cocoa powder, marshmallows

---

## Instructions

1. **Chocolate base.** Squeeze **30g of Monin Dark Chocolate Sauce** into a pre-warmed mug. Swirl to coat the bottom.
2. **Steam the milk.** Steam **280ml** of whole milk to **60–65°C** (or **50–55°C for children**). Create smooth, velvety microfoam — more foam is welcome here.
3. **Pour and stir.** Pour the steamed milk into the chocolate sauce. Stir well from the bottom with a long spoon until the chocolate sauce is fully dissolved.
4. **Top as desired.** Add whipped cream, a dusting of cocoa powder, or marshmallows for a premium presentation.
5. Serve immediately.

---

## Pro-Tips

- **Stir from the bottom** — dark chocolate sauce is dense and sinks. Under-stirred hot chocolate will have a bitter chocolate deposit at the bottom.
- For a **richer hot chocolate**, increase sauce to **40g** or use Monin Dark Chocolate Sauce with a splash of Monin Vanilla Syrup (10ml).
- For a **Mexican Hot Chocolate** effect, add a small pinch of cinnamon and cayenne pepper on top.
- **Children's temperature: 50–55°C** — test with a thermometer. Never serve above 65°C to children.
