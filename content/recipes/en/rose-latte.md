# Rose Latte

A beautifully scented latte with the classic and romantic taste of rose.

---

## Ingredients
- 1 shot (30 ml) Gia or Miscela Bar espresso
- 15 ml Monin Rose Syrup
- 200 ml steamed milk
- Edible dried rose petals for garnish

---

## Instructions
1. Extract a single shot of Gia or Miscela Bar espresso into a cup.
2. Pour the Monin Rose Syrup into the espresso and mix well.
3. Steam the milk to a silky microfoam consistency.
4. Gently pour the steamed milk over the espresso base.
5. Garnish with a few edible dried rose petals.

---

## Pro-Tips
- Ensure the espresso is extracted perfectly to balance the sweetness of the rose syrup.
- Monin Rose Syrup can be potent; adjust the dosage slightly if a milder flavor is preferred.
