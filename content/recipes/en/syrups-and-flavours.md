# Syrups & Flavours Guide

Parista's flavour system is built on the **Monin syrup and sauce range** — the industry standard for consistency, quality, and variety. This guide documents every available flavour, how to use it, and what to combine it with.

---

## Standard Amount

| Format | Amount |
|---|---|
| **Syrup in hot drinks** | 15–20ml |
| **Syrup in cold/iced drinks** | 15–20ml (add to base before ice) |
| **Sauce in hot drinks** | 20–30g |
| **Sauce in blended drinks** | 25–35g |

> **Always add syrup to the cup before espresso** — the hot espresso dissolves and integrates the syrup immediately.

## Pro-Tips for Flavour Work

- **Taste before serving.** If you add a new syrup combination, taste it before it goes to the customer.
- **Sweet balance:** Fruity syrups (mango, raspberry) pair better with lighter milk drinks. Caramel and nut syrups pair better with bold espresso bases.
- **Temperature matters:** Some flavours (lavender, rose) are more delicate hot — reduce to 10ml in hot drinks and keep at 15ml in cold.
- **Upsell syrups:** "Would you like to add a flavour to your latte?" — always mention this. Syrups are a high-margin addition with minimal effort.
