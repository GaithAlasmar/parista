# Syphon Coffee
A theatrical and precise full-immersion brewing method that yields a clean, tea-like cup with delicate flavors.

---

## Ingredients
- 20g of medium-fine ground coffee
- 300ml of hot water (approx. 90-95°C)
- 1:15 coffee to water ratio

---

## Instructions
1. Secure the filter to the upper chamber (hopper) and hook it in place.
2. Pour 300ml of hot water into the lower globe.
3. Turn on the heat source and place it under the globe. Wait for the water to boil.
4. Once boiling, insert the upper chamber into the globe. The water will be drawn up.
5. When most of the water is in the top chamber, reduce the heat slightly and add the 20g of ground coffee.
6. Gently stir to ensure all coffee grounds are saturated and start a timer.
7. Let it brew for 1 minute to 1 minute and 30 seconds.
8. Remove the heat source. The brewed coffee will draw back down into the lower globe.
9. Wait for the coffee to cool slightly before serving.

---

## Pro-Tips
- Ensure the filter is centered and tightly secured to avoid grounds escaping into the final brew.
- Do not let the water temperature go too high in the upper chamber; it should be around 90-94°C for optimal extraction.
