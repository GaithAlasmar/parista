# Toffee Nut Latte

A rich, buttery, and nutty latte that feels like a cozy hug.

---

## Ingredients
- 1 shot (30 ml) Gia or Miscela Bar espresso
- 15 ml Monin Toffee Nut Syrup
- 200 ml steamed milk
- Whipped cream (optional)
- Crushed nuts or toffee bits for garnish

---

## Instructions
1. Pull a single shot of Gia or Miscela Bar espresso into a serving cup.
2. Stir in the Monin Toffee Nut Syrup until well combined.
3. Steam the milk to a velvety texture with moderate foam.
4. Pour the steamed milk over the espresso base.
5. Top with whipped cream and garnish with crushed nuts or toffee bits if desired.

---

## Pro-Tips
- This flavor profile works particularly well with the deeper chocolate notes of Miscela Bar espresso.
- For a richer treat, drizzle a little caramel sauce over the whipped cream.
