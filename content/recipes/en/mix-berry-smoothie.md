# Mix Berry Smoothie

A deep ruby-red berry slush combining **Monin Raspberry Syrup** and **Monin Strawberry Syrup** — blended with ice in the **Sonifer blender** to a thick, refreshing consistency. Two berries in one cup, double the flavour. No dairy, no coffee.

---

## Ingredients

- 1 cup ice (approx. 200 g)
- 80 ml cold water *(splash — just enough to blend)*
- 20 ml **Monin Raspberry Syrup**
- 20 ml **Monin Strawberry Syrup**

---

## Method

1. **Load the Sonifer blender:**
   - 80 ml cold water *(first — protects blades)*
   - 20 ml **Monin Raspberry Syrup**
   - 20 ml **Monin Strawberry Syrup**
   - 200 g ice *(last — on top)*
2. **Blend on high** for **25–35 seconds** until a smooth, uniform dark-pink slush forms with no ice chunks.
3. **Consistency check:** Thick and slushy — should pour slowly. Too thick → add 20 ml water and pulse 5 s. Too thin → add more ice and re-blend 10 s.
4. **Pour into cup.** The colour should be a vivid ruby-pink.
5. Serve immediately with a straw.

---

## Flavour Notes

| Monin Syrup | Character |
|---|---|
| Raspberry | Tart, slightly jammy, deep red |
| Strawberry | Sweet, bright, candy-like |

> Combined, they create a balanced berry profile — neither too tart nor too sweet.

---

## Tips

- For a **stronger raspberry** taste: use 30 ml raspberry + 10 ml strawberry.
- Add a splash of **Monin Lemon Syrup** (5 ml) for a berry-lemonade variant.
- Dairy-free, caffeine-free. One of the most visually striking drinks on the menu.
