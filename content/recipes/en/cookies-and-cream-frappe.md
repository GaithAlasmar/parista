# Cookies & Cream Frappé

A creamy, cookie-filled delight blended to frosty perfection.

---

## Ingredients
- 3 whole chocolate sandwich cookies (like Oreo)
- 20 ml Monin Vanilla Syrup
- 120 ml whole milk
- 1 scoop (20 g) vanilla frappe base powder
- 200 g ice cubes
- Optional: 1 shot (30 ml) espresso (Gia or Miscela Bar) if requested with coffee
- Whipped cream (for garnish)
- Crushed cookie crumbs (for garnish)

---

## Instructions
1. Break the cookies in half and place them into the Sonifer commercial blender.
2. Add the milk, Monin Vanilla Syrup, vanilla frappe base powder, ice cubes, and the espresso shot (if using).
3. Blend using the Sonifer commercial blender on high for about 30-40 seconds until the mixture is thick, creamy, and the cookies are broken down into small bits.
4. Pour the mixture into a serving glass.
5. Top with whipped cream and a dusting of crushed cookie crumbs.

---

## Pro-Tips
- Do not over-blend; you want visible cookie crumbles in the frappé for the best texture.
- For a more intense chocolate flavor, add a pump of Monin Chocolate Syrup.
