# Chocolate Milkshake

Deep, fudgy, and intensely chocolatey — **3 scoops vanilla ice cream** blended with whole milk and **Monin Dark Chocolate Sauce** in the **Sonifer blender**. The sauce melts into the ice cream to create a genuinely rich chocolate flavour rather than a thin, watered-down shake.

---

## Ingredients

- 3 large scoops vanilla ice cream (~180 g)
- 80 ml whole milk *(splash — just enough to blend)*
- 30 ml **Monin Dark Chocolate Sauce**
- Whipped cream — for topping
- Extra **Monin Dark Chocolate Sauce** — drizzle for topping

---

## Method

1. **Drizzle the cup.** Before pouring, drizzle a spiral of **Monin Dark Chocolate Sauce** on the inner cup wall — it sticks and creates a visual effect.
2. **Load the Sonifer blender:**
   - 80 ml whole milk *(first)*
   - 30 ml **Monin Dark Chocolate Sauce**
   - 3 scoops vanilla ice cream *(last)*
3. **Blend on medium-high** for **20–30 seconds** until smooth and evenly chocolatey brown. Do not over-blend.
4. **Consistency check:** Should be thick and slow-pouring. If too thick, add 15 ml milk and pulse 5 s.
5. **Pour into prepared cup.**
6. **Top with whipped cream.**
7. Finish with a **Monin Dark Chocolate Sauce** drizzle over the cream.

---

## Key Rules for Milkshakes

> ⚠️ **No ice.** Ice cream is the chilling agent — ice dilutes and ruins the texture.

- Use the sauce inside the blender (not just as a topping) for full chocolate flavour throughout.
- Serve immediately after blending.
