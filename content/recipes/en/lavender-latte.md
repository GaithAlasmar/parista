# Lavender Latte

A soothing and aromatic latte infused with delicate lavender syrup.

---

## Ingredients
- 1 shot (30 ml) Gia or Miscela Bar espresso
- 15 ml Monin Lavender Syrup
- 200 ml steamed milk
- Dried lavender buds for garnish (optional)

---

## Instructions
1. Brew a single shot of Gia or Miscela Bar espresso into a serving cup.
2. Add the Monin Lavender Syrup to the hot espresso and stir gently.
3. Steam the milk until it reaches a smooth, velvety texture.
4. Pour the steamed milk over the espresso and syrup mixture.
5. Garnish with a sprinkle of dried lavender buds if desired.

---

## Pro-Tips
- Don't overheat the milk, as it can mask the subtle floral notes of the lavender.
- For an iced version, use cold milk and pour over ice after mixing the espresso and syrup.
