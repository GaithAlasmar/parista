# Caramel Frappe

A rich, indulgent blended drink built on **Monin Caramel Syrup**, **Monin Caramel Sauce**, a double shot of espresso from our **Gia coffee beans**, and the **Sonifer commercial blender** — delivering consistent, cafe-quality results every time.

---

## Ingredients (Single 16 oz / 480 ml Serving)

| Ingredient | Amount |
|---|---|
| Frappe Base Powder | 30g - 40g (1.5 - 2 scoops) |
| Ice (crushed or cubed) | 250 g (approx. 2 cups) |
| Whole milk (or oat milk) | 150 ml |
| Double espresso — Gia beans | 60 ml (2 shots) |
| **Monin Caramel Syrup** | 30 ml (2 tbsp) |
| **Monin Caramel Sauce** (blend + topping) | 15 ml in blend + 10 ml for topping |
| Whipped cream | as needed for topping |
| Pinch of flaky sea salt (optional — salted caramel) | 1 small pinch |

---

## Equipment

- **Sonifer commercial blender** — use the high-speed blend cycle
- 16 oz cold cup with dome lid
- Whipped cream dispenser or can
- **Monin Caramel Sauce** squeeze bottle
- Precision scale (recommended for consistency across shifts)

---

## Step-by-Step Instructions

### Step 1 – Pull & Cool the Espresso
- Pull a **double espresso shot (60 ml)** using freshly calibrated Gia beans.
- Allow to cool **1–2 minutes**, OR pour over a small handful of ice in a side cup and stir to flash-chill.
- **Do not** add hot espresso directly into the Sonifer — it melts ice unevenly and thins the texture.

> *During peak service:* Use **60 ml of cold brew concentrate** (1:4 ratio) made from Gia beans for a smoother, less acidic base — faster build, lower extraction stress on the Casadio.

### Step 2 – Load the Sonifer Blender
Add in this exact order to protect the blades and ensure even blending:

1. **150 ml whole milk** (first — protects blades)
2. **Frappe Base Powder (30g - 40g)** (adds texture and prevents separation)
2. **30 ml Monin Caramel Syrup**
3. **15 ml Monin Caramel Sauce**
4. **60 ml cooled espresso** (Gia beans)
5. **250 g ice** (last — on top)

### Step 3 – Blend
- Set the **Sonifer to high speed for 30–45 seconds** until the mixture is smooth, uniform, and pourable — no visible ice chunks.
- **Consistency check:** The frappe should coat the back of a spoon. If too thick, add 20–30 ml milk and pulse 5 seconds. If too thin, add a handful of ice and re-blend 10 seconds.

> **Batch prep (busy periods):** Pre-mix milk + Monin Caramel Syrup + cold brew in a pitcher and refrigerate. Per order: add 240 ml pre-mix + 250 g ice and blend.

### Step 4 – Pour & Garnish
- Drizzle a thin **Monin Caramel Sauce** spiral on the inner cup wall before pouring (visible caramel swirl effect).
- Pour the blended frappe, leaving **3–4 cm** headspace.
- Add a generous mound of **whipped cream** in a circular motion.
- Drizzle **10 ml Monin Caramel Sauce** in a crosshatch or spiral over the cream.
- *(Optional)* Finish with a **pinch of flaky sea salt** for a salted caramel variant.

### Step 5 – Lid & Serve
- Cap with a **dome lid** to protect the cream presentation.
- Serve immediately .

---

## Quality Control Checklist

- [ ] Ice fully blended — no visible chunks
- [ ] Colour is consistent caramel-tan (no white milk streaks)
- [ ] Whipped cream holds its dome shape
- [ ] Monin Caramel Sauce swirl visible through cup wall
- [ ] Drink temperature below 5 °C

---

## Variations

| Variant | Modification |
|---|---|
| Salted Caramel | Add 1 pinch flaky sea salt + Monin Caramel Sauce rim |
| Decaf | Replace Gia espresso with decaf shots |
| Dairy-Free | Replace milk with oat milk at 1:1 ratio |
| Extra Strong | 3 shots Gia espresso (90 ml) + reduce milk to 120 ml |

---

> **Storage Note:** Always build to order. Pre-blend base (without ice) can be refrigerated for up to **8 hours** during a shift.
