# Nitro Cold Brew

Cold Brew elevated to its highest form. **Nitrogen gas is infused into Cold Brew concentrate**, creating thousands of micro-bubbles that produce a thick, velvety, creamy texture — similar to a nitro stout beer. No milk, no ice, no sugar. Just coffee and nitrogen.

---

## Ingredients

- **350ml** Cold Brew concentrate (see Cold Brew recipe)
- Nitrogen gas (via keg system, Nitro whipper, or nitrogen cartridges)
- Nitro tap or nitrogen infusion device

---

## Instructions

1. **Prepare Cold Brew concentrate.** Follow the Cold Brew recipe — 100g coffee to 1 litre water, 18 hours steep. Use full strength (undiluted) for Nitro.
2. **Load the system.** Transfer cold brew concentrate into the keg, Nitro whipper, or infusion vessel. Charge with nitrogen gas according to your equipment's specifications.
3. **Allow to infuse.** Let the nitrogen infuse for at least **30 minutes** (longer is better — overnight in the keg is ideal).
4. **Pour.** Pour slowly from the tap or dispense from the whipper into a clear glass. Do NOT add ice — the cold brew is served at refrigerator temperature.
5. **Watch the cascade.** As the Nitro is poured, thousands of tiny nitrogen bubbles cascade down the glass before settling into a creamy, stout-like head of foam.
6. Serve immediately — the nitrogen head disappears within minutes.

---

## Pro-Tips

- **No ice, no straw** — Nitro Cold Brew is served straight in a clear glass so the cascade effect is visible.
- The nitrogen gives a **naturally sweet perception** on the palate — no sugar or sweetener should be needed.
- **Glassware matters:** Use a clear, slightly tulip-shaped glass (similar to a beer glass) to enhance the cascade visual effect.
- If using a Nitro whipper (iSi-style), shake gently after charging and dispense slowly at a slight angle.
