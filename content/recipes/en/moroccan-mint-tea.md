# Moroccan Mint Tea
A traditional, refreshing blend of green tea and fresh mint leaves, lightly sweetened for a soothing experience.

---
## Ingredients
- 5g Gunpowder Green Tea
- 15g Fresh Mint Leaves
- 400ml Hot Water (90°C)
- 20ml Monin Cane Sugar Syrup

---
## Instructions
1. Rinse the green tea leaves with a little hot water and discard the water to remove bitterness.
2. Place the rinsed tea leaves, fresh mint leaves, and Monin Cane Sugar Syrup into a teapot.
3. Pour the hot water into the teapot.
4. Let the tea steep for about 5 minutes.
5. Pour the tea into a glass from a height to aerate it and create a slight foam on the surface.
6. Serve hot, garnished with a sprig of fresh mint.

---
## Pro-Tips
- Pouring from a height is crucial for the authentic Moroccan experience, as it aerates the tea and enhances the flavor.
- Adjust the amount of Monin Cane Sugar Syrup according to guest preference.
