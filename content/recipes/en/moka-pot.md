# Moka Pot
A classic Italian stovetop method for brewing strong, espresso-like coffee with a rich and robust flavor profile.

---

## Ingredients
- 15g of fine ground coffee (slightly coarser than espresso)
- 150ml of pre-heated water
- Approximately 1:10 coffee to water ratio

---

## Instructions
1. Pour pre-heated water into the bottom chamber up to just below the safety valve.
2. Insert the filter basket and fill it evenly with the ground coffee. Level it off without tamping or pressing down.
3. Screw the top and bottom chambers together tightly using a towel to hold the hot bottom chamber.
4. Place the Moka pot on a stovetop over medium heat.
5. Leave the lid open to monitor the extraction. Coffee will begin to emerge from the spout.
6. When the flow becomes pale and starts to bubble or sputter, immediately remove the pot from the heat.
7. Run the bottom chamber under cold water or wrap it in a cold towel to stop the extraction instantly.
8. Pour into a cup and enjoy.

---

## Pro-Tips
- Always use hot water to start; starting with cold water can heat the coffee grounds too much before extraction, causing bitterness.
- Never tamp the coffee in the basket; it can create too much pressure and be dangerous.
