# Pistachio Frappé

A nutty and creamy blended beverage featuring rich pistachio flavor.

---

## Ingredients
- 1 shot (30 ml) espresso (Gia or Miscela Bar)
- 20 ml Monin Pistachio Syrup (or pistachio sauce)
- 10 ml Monin Vanilla Syrup
- 100 ml whole milk
- 1 scoop (20 g) vanilla frappe base powder
- 200 g ice cubes
- Whipped cream (for garnish)
- Crushed pistachios (for garnish)

---

## Instructions
1. Add the ice cubes, whole milk, espresso, Monin Pistachio Syrup, Monin Vanilla Syrup, and vanilla frappe base powder into the Sonifer commercial blender.
2. Secure the lid on the Sonifer commercial blender and blend on high for 30-45 seconds until the texture is smooth and thick.
3. Pour the blended mixture into a tall serving glass.
4. Top with a swirl of whipped cream and generously sprinkle crushed pistachios on top.

---

## Pro-Tips
- Combining Monin Pistachio Syrup with a touch of Monin Vanilla Syrup rounds out the flavor and gives it a dessert-like profile.
- You can rim the glass with a little pistachio sauce and crushed nuts for a premium presentation.
