# Banana Smoothie
A rich and creamy classic banana smoothie.

---
## Ingredients
- 1.5 ripe bananas
- 20ml Monin Banana Puree or Vanilla Syrup
- 150ml milk
- 100g ice cubes

---
## Instructions
1. Place the bananas, Monin Banana Puree (or Vanilla Syrup), milk, and ice into the Sonifer commercial blender.
2. Blend on high speed for 30-45 seconds until completely smooth.
3. Pour into a tall glass and serve.

---
## Pro-Tips
- A dash of cinnamon on top adds a nice aromatic touch.
- For a richer taste, use full-fat milk or add a scoop of vanilla ice cream.
