# Lemon Mint Mojito
A classic and refreshing combination of zesty lemon and cool mint, perfect for hot days.

---
## Ingredients
- 20 ml Monin Mojito Mint Syrup
- 10 ml Monin Lemon Syrup (or fresh lemon juice)
- 8-10 fresh mint leaves
- 3 lemon wedges
- 200 ml sparkling water or club soda
- 150 g ice cubes

---
## Instructions
1. In a sturdy glass, muddle the fresh mint leaves and lemon wedges together to release the flavors.
2. Add the Monin Mojito Mint Syrup and Monin Lemon Syrup to the glass.
3. Fill the glass to the top with ice cubes.
4. Top up with sparkling water or club soda.
5. Stir gently from the bottom up to mix the syrups with the water.
6. Garnish with a fresh sprig of mint and a lemon slice.

---
## Pro-Tips
- "Slap" the mint leaves before garnishing to release the essential oils for a stronger aroma.
- Do not over-muddle the mint, as it can make the drink bitter. Press gently just to release the oils.
