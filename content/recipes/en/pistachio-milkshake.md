# Pistachio Milkshake
A deliciously nutty and vibrant pistachio milkshake.

## Ingredients
- 150 ml cold whole milk
- 3 scoops (150 g) premium vanilla ice cream
- 30 ml Monin Pistachio syrup
- 15 g pistachio paste or spread
- 10 g crushed pistachios (for garnish)
- Whipped cream (for garnish)

## Instructions
1. Add the cold whole milk, vanilla ice cream, Monin Pistachio syrup, and pistachio paste into a Sonifer commercial blender.
2. Blend on medium-high speed for 30-45 seconds until the mixture has a smooth and velvety texture.
3. Pour the bright green milkshake into a 400 ml glass.
4. Top generously with whipped cream.
5. Sprinkle crushed pistachios over the whipped cream for a crunchy finish.

## Pro-Tips
- Adding a pinch of sea salt enhances the roasted nut profile of the pistachio.
- Use fresh, high-quality pistachio paste for the best natural green color.
