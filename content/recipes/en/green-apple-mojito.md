# Green Apple Mojito
A crisp and tart variation of the classic mojito, featuring the vibrant taste of green apple.

---
## Ingredients
- 20 ml Monin Green Apple Syrup
- 10 ml Monin Mojito Mint Syrup
- 8 fresh mint leaves
- 2 lime wedges
- 200 ml sparkling water
- 150 g ice cubes
- Thin green apple slices for garnish

---
## Instructions
1. Muddle the fresh mint leaves and lime wedges gently in a serving glass.
2. Pour in the Monin Green Apple Syrup and Monin Mojito Mint Syrup.
3. Fill the glass completely with ice cubes.
4. Top with sparkling water.
5. Stir gently to incorporate the syrups evenly.
6. Garnish with thin slices of green apple and a sprig of mint.

---
## Pro-Tips
- Use a tall highball glass to showcase the beautiful green color of the drink.
- Keep the green apple garnish crisp by slicing it just before serving.
