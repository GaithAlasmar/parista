# Cinnamon Latte

A warm and comforting latte featuring the spicy-sweet notes of cinnamon.

---

## Ingredients
- 1 shot (30 ml) Gia or Miscela Bar espresso
- 15 ml Monin Cinnamon Syrup
- 200 ml steamed milk
- Cinnamon powder for dusting

---

## Instructions
1. Prepare a single shot of Gia or Miscela Bar espresso in a mug.
2. Add the Monin Cinnamon Syrup to the hot espresso.
3. Steam the milk until it is hot and frothy.
4. Pour the milk into the mug, mixing it with the flavored espresso.
5. Dust the top with a pinch of cinnamon powder.

---

## Pro-Tips
- Steaming a slight sprinkle of cinnamon powder along with the milk can enhance the aroma.
- Pairs wonderfully with oat milk for a cozy, vegan-friendly alternative.
