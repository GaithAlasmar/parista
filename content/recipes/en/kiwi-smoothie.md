# Kiwi Smoothie
A tangy, vibrant, and incredibly refreshing kiwi smoothie.

---
## Ingredients
- 2 ripe kiwis, peeled
- 20ml Monin Kiwi Puree or Green Apple Syrup
- 100ml apple juice or water
- 150g ice cubes

---
## Instructions
1. Add the peeled kiwis, Monin Kiwi Puree, apple juice (or water), and ice into the Sonifer commercial blender.
2. Blend on high speed for 30 seconds. Do not over-blend to avoid crushing the seeds too much, which can make it bitter.
3. Pour into a chilled serving glass.

---
## Pro-Tips
- Leave a slice of kiwi with the skin on for an attractive garnish on the rim of the glass.
- Combine with a bit of banana or strawberry for a balanced flavor profile.
