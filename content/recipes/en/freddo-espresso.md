# Freddo Espresso

A Greek cafe staple. Freddo Espresso is pure coffee and ice, whipped in a drink mixer or shaker to create a refreshing, intensely flavoured cold coffee with a thick, frothy head.

---

## Ingredients

- **60 ml** double espresso (two shots) — **Gia beans**
- **10-15 ml** simple syrup or liquid sugar (adjust to customer preference)
- **200 g** ice for shaking
- **Fresh ice** for serving

---

## Instructions

1. **Pull the espresso:** Extract a double shot (60 ml) of Gia espresso into a small pitcher.
2. **Sweeten:** Add the liquid sugar to the hot espresso and stir briefly. (If the customer prefers it unsweetened, skip this step).
3. **Blend/Shake:** Pour the sweetened espresso into a milkshake mixer cup (or shaker) along with 2-3 medium ice cubes.
4. **Mix:** Spin on the drink mixer for 10-15 seconds (or shake vigorously) until the ice shatters and a thick, dense foam is created.
5. **Serve:** Fill a glass with fresh ice and strain the frothy espresso over the ice.

---

## Pro-Tips

- **Use liquid sugar:** Granulated sugar won't dissolve properly in a cold drink. Always mix liquid sugar with the hot espresso before adding ice.
- **Avoid over-dilution:** Only use 2-3 ice cubes in the mixer. Too much ice will water down the espresso and ruin the texture of the foam.
- **Serve immediately:** The foam is stable but will begin to separate over time. It should be served as soon as it is poured.
