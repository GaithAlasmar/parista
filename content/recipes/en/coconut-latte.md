# Coconut Latte

A tropical twist on the classic latte, featuring the sweet, creamy taste of coconut.

---

## Ingredients
- 1 shot (30 ml) Gia or Miscela Bar espresso
- 15 ml Monin Coconut Syrup
- 200 ml steamed milk (dairy or plant-based)
- Toasted coconut flakes for garnish

---

## Instructions
1. Brew a fresh shot of Gia or Miscela Bar espresso into your cup.
2. Mix in the Monin Coconut Syrup.
3. Steam the milk to a creamy consistency with slight foam.
4. Pour the steamed milk over the espresso and syrup mixture.
5. Garnish with a sprinkle of toasted coconut flakes.

---

## Pro-Tips
- For an ultimate tropical experience, use coconut milk instead of regular dairy milk.
- Make sure to toast the coconut flakes lightly; it adds a pleasant crunch and aroma.
