# Hazelnut Latte

A warm, nutty classic — **espresso and steamed milk enriched with 15ml of Monin Hazelnut Syrup**. The hazelnut's toasty, earthy sweetness elevates the espresso's roasted notes into something deeply comforting. One of the most consistently popular flavoured lattes.

---

## Ingredients

- **30ml** single espresso (9g) — **Gia beans**
- **15ml** Monin Hazelnut Syrup
- **180ml** whole milk, steamed to silky microfoam

---

## Instructions

1. **Hazelnut base.** Pour **15ml Monin Hazelnut Syrup** into the bottom of a pre-warmed cup.
2. **Pull the espresso.** Extract **9g** and pour directly over the syrup. Stir briefly.
3. **Steam the milk.** Steam **220ml** of whole milk to **60–65°C** with smooth, velvety microfoam.
4. **Pour.** Pour the steamed milk into the hazelnut-espresso base. Latte art as desired.
5. Serve immediately.

---

## Pro-Tips

- **Hazelnut + Vanilla** (10ml each) creates the beloved "Nutty Vanilla Latte" — one of the most requested off-menu combinations.
- **Hazelnut + Chocolate Sauce** creates a homemade "Nutella Latte" effect — suggest this as a premium upgrade.
- For an **Iced Hazelnut Latte:** syrup → ice → cold milk → espresso float.
- **Gia beans** work best here — their bold, slightly bitter profile provides the counterbalance to hazelnut's natural sweetness.
