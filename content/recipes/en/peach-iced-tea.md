# Peach Iced Tea

A highly requested summer refresher. Black tea forms a robust base, perfectly balanced by the sweet, fruity notes of Monin Peach Syrup.

---

## Ingredients

- **150 ml** strongly brewed black tea (cooled)
- **20 ml** Monin Peach Syrup
- **10 ml** fresh lemon juice (optional, for balance)
- **200 g** ice
- Fresh peach slices and mint leaves for garnish

---

## Instructions

1. **Brew the tea base:** Steep black tea bags in hot water for 4-5 minutes, remove bags, and let cool. (Keep a batch of concentrated black tea in the fridge for service).
2. **Combine:** In a shaker, add 150 ml of the chilled black tea, 20 ml of Monin Peach Syrup, and 10 ml of fresh lemon juice.
3. **Shake:** Add a scoop of ice and shake well for 5-10 seconds to chill and mix thoroughly.
4. **Serve:** Pour the entire mixture into a tall serving glass.
5. **Garnish:** Top with a slice of fresh peach and a sprig of mint.

---

## Pro-Tips

- **Tea concentration:** Ensure the black tea is brewed strong. When shaken with ice and syrup, a weak tea base will taste watery.
- **Acidity balance:** The dash of fresh lemon juice cuts through the sweetness of the peach syrup, making the drink much more refreshing.
- **Batching:** Brew 1-2 liters of black tea at the start of the shift to handle rapid orders efficiently.
