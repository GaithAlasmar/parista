# Iced Matcha Latte

A vibrant, earthy, and highly requested non-coffee alternative. This premium drink uses high-quality matcha powder, sweetened perfectly and layered over cold milk and ice.

---

## Ingredients

- **3 g** premium matcha powder
- **30 ml** hot water (80°C - do not use boiling water)
- **15 ml** Monin Vanilla Syrup (or honey)
- **180 ml** cold whole milk (or oat milk)
- **200 g** ice

---

## Instructions

1. **Prepare the matcha shot:** Sift 3 g of matcha powder into a small bowl or cup.
2. **Whisk:** Add 30 ml of hot water (approx. 80°C). Whisk vigorously using a bamboo chasen or small electric frother in an "M" or "W" motion until no lumps remain and a light froth forms.
3. **Sweeten:** Stir 15 ml of Monin Vanilla Syrup into the matcha shot.
4. **Prepare the glass:** Fill a serving glass with ice and pour in 180 ml of cold milk.
5. **Layer:** Slowly pour the sweetened matcha shot over the cold milk and ice to create a distinct green-over-white visual layer.

---

## Pro-Tips

- **Water temperature matters:** Using boiling water (100°C) will scorch the matcha, making it taste bitter and fishy. Let the hot water cool slightly to 80°C before mixing.
- **Sifting is crucial:** Un-sifted matcha will form unpleasant clumps that won't dissolve.
- **Layering technique:** Pour the matcha directly over an ice cube at the top of the glass to soften the pour and maintain the beautiful separation of colors.
