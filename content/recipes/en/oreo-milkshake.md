# Oreo Milkshake
A classic creamy milkshake blended with Oreo cookies.

## Ingredients
- 150 ml cold whole milk
- 3 scoops (150 g) premium vanilla ice cream
- 4 Oreo cookies (crushed)
- 15 ml Monin Vanilla syrup
- 10 ml Monin Dark Chocolate sauce (for drizzling)
- Whipped cream (for garnish)
- 1 extra Oreo cookie (for garnish)

## Instructions
1. Drizzle the Monin Dark Chocolate sauce inside a chilled 400 ml serving glass.
2. Add the cold whole milk, vanilla ice cream, 4 crushed Oreo cookies, and Monin Vanilla syrup into a Sonifer commercial blender.
3. Blend on medium-high speed for 30-40 seconds until smooth and creamy.
4. Pour the blended mixture into the prepared glass.
5. Top generously with whipped cream and garnish with the extra Oreo cookie.

## Pro-Tips
- For a thicker consistency, freeze the milk into cubes before blending.
- Avoid over-blending, or the milkshake will become too watery.
