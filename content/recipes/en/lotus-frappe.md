# Lotus Frappé

A sweet, spiced caramel frappé made with Lotus Biscoff spread and cookies.

---

## Ingredients
- 1 shot (30 ml) espresso (Gia or Miscela Bar)
- 30 g Lotus Biscoff Spread (or 20 ml Monin Caramel Sauce as an alternative base)
- 2 Lotus Biscoff cookies
- 100 ml whole milk
- 1 scoop (20 g) vanilla frappe base powder
- 200 g ice cubes
- Whipped cream (for garnish)
- 1 extra Lotus cookie and crushed Lotus crumbs (for garnish)
- Monin Caramel Sauce (for drizzle)

---

## Instructions
1. Break the 2 Lotus cookies into halves and drop them into the Sonifer commercial blender.
2. Add the Lotus Biscoff spread, espresso, whole milk, vanilla frappe base powder, and ice cubes.
3. Blend using the Sonifer commercial blender on high for 30-40 seconds until the mixture is fully incorporated and icy.
4. Drizzle the inside of your serving glass with Monin Caramel Sauce.
5. Pour the blended frappé into the glass.
6. Top with whipped cream, an extra Lotus cookie, and a sprinkle of crushed Lotus crumbs.

---

## Pro-Tips
- Slightly warming the Lotus spread before adding it to the blender ensures it mixes evenly with the cold ingredients.
- The Gia or Miscela Bar espresso shot balances the intense sweetness of the Lotus spread perfectly.
