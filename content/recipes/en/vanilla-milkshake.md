# Vanilla Milkshake

Pure, creamy, and classic — **3 scoops of vanilla ice cream** blended with whole milk and a splash of **Monin Vanilla Syrup** in the **Sonifer blender**. No ice. Thick. Indulgent.

---

## Ingredients

- 3 large scoops vanilla ice cream (~180 g)
- 80 ml whole milk *(splash — just enough to blend)*
- 20 ml **Monin Vanilla Syrup**
- Whipped cream — for topping
- Sprinkles or wafer straw — optional garnish

---

## Method

1. **Load the Sonifer blender:**
   - 80 ml whole milk *(add first to protect blades)*
   - 20 ml **Monin Vanilla Syrup**
   - 3 scoops vanilla ice cream
2. **Blend on medium-high** for **20–30 seconds** — do NOT over-blend. The milkshake should be thick and pourable but not runny.
3. **Consistency check:** Should be very thick — the shake should slowly pour, not flow freely. If too thick to pour, add 20 ml milk and pulse 5 s.
4. **Pour into cup.** The shake should stay stacked and hold its shape slightly.
5. **Top with whipped cream** — pile it high.
6. Optional: finish with a wafer straw, sprinkles, or a light dusting of vanilla powder.

---

## Key Rules for Milkshakes

> ⚠️ **No ice in milkshakes.** Ice cream is the chilling agent. Ice would dilute the cream and destroy the thick texture.

- Keep ice cream scoops in the freezer until the moment of blending.
- Use cold milk straight from the fridge.
- Serve immediately — milkshakes separate and thin out within 5 minutes.
