# Spanish Latte

A crowd-favourite fusion of bold **Gia** (or **Miscela Bar**) espresso, sweet white mocha sauce, and silky cold milk — served long over ice. The white mocha gives it that signature creamy sweetness without overpowering the espresso.

---

## Ingredients

- 250 ml ice (large cup)
- 150 ml whole milk
- 2 shots (60 ml) double espresso — **Gia beans**
- 20 ml **Monin White Chocolate (White Mocha) Sauce**
- 10 ml **Monin Vanilla Syrup** *(optional — for extra sweetness)*

---

## Method

1. **Pull the espresso.** Extract a double shot (60 ml) from freshly ground **Gia beans**. Set aside to cool slightly.
2. **Sauce first.** Add **20 ml Monin White Mocha Sauce** to the bottom of the cup — swirl to coat.
3. **Add vanilla syrup** (if using): pour **10 ml Monin Vanilla Syrup** over the sauce.
4. **Fill with ice.** Pack the cup with ice cubes.
5. **Pour the milk.** Add **150 ml whole milk** over the ice — it will mix with the sauce on the way down.
6. **Float the espresso.** Slowly pour the double espresso shot over the back of a spoon so it layers on top for a visual gradient.
7. **Stir before drinking** — or serve as-is for the layered presentation.

---

## Tips

- For a **hot Spanish Latte**: skip the ice, steam the milk to 60–65 °C, and stir the sauce directly in before adding the milk.
- Use **Miscela Bar** beans for a milder, sweeter espresso base that blends beautifully with the white mocha.
- Add a dusting of **cinnamon or cocoa powder** on top for a finishing touch.
