# Freddo Cappuccino

The indulgent cousin of the Freddo Espresso. It features the same shaken, frothy iced espresso base but is crowned with a thick layer of cold skimmed milk foam that slowly cascades into the coffee.

---

## Ingredients

- **60 ml** double espresso — **Gia beans**
- **10-15 ml** liquid sugar or simple syrup
- **100 ml** cold skimmed milk (must be very cold and 0% or 1% fat)
- **Ice** for shaking and serving
- Cocoa powder or cinnamon for garnish

---

## Instructions

1. **Prepare the Freddo Espresso base:** Extract the double espresso, mix with liquid sugar, and shake or spin with 2-3 ice cubes until thick and frothy.
2. **Pour the base:** Strain the frothy espresso over fresh ice in a tall serving glass, leaving room at the top for the milk foam.
3. **Prepare the cold foam:** In a clean milkshake mixer cup or using a handheld frother, whip the 100 ml of cold skimmed milk until it becomes thick, glossy, and doubles in volume.
4. **Top the drink:** Slowly spoon or pour the thick cold foam over the iced espresso layer.
5. **Garnish:** Dust lightly with cocoa powder or cinnamon.

---

## Pro-Tips

- **Skimmed milk is essential:** Whole milk will not whip into the stiff, stable cold foam required for a proper Freddo Cappuccino. You must use extremely cold skimmed (non-fat) milk.
- **Patience with the foam:** The foam should be thick enough that it rests neatly on top of the espresso without immediately mixing in.
- **Straw protocol:** Serve with a straw so the customer can drink the coffee through the foam layer.
