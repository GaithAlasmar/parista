# Java Chip Frappé

A rich and icy blend of coffee, chocolate chips, and mocha sauce.

---

## Ingredients
- 1 shot (30 ml) espresso (Gia or Miscela Bar)
- 30 ml Monin Dark Chocolate Sauce
- 20 g chocolate chips
- 100 ml whole milk
- 1 scoop (20 g) frappe base powder (vanilla or neutral)
- 200 g ice cubes
- Whipped cream (for garnish)
- Extra chocolate chips and Monin Dark Chocolate Sauce (for drizzle)

---

## Instructions
1. Add the ice cubes, milk, espresso, Monin Dark Chocolate Sauce, frappe base powder, and chocolate chips into the Sonifer commercial blender pitcher.
2. Secure the lid on the Sonifer commercial blender and blend on high for 30-45 seconds until smooth and icy.
3. Drizzle the inside of your serving glass with Monin Dark Chocolate Sauce.
4. Pour the blended frappé into the prepared glass.
5. Top with a generous swirl of whipped cream, an extra drizzle of mocha sauce, and a sprinkle of chocolate chips.

---

## Pro-Tips
- To prevent the chocolate chips from completely pulverizing, you can pulse the blender a few times at the end of the blending cycle instead of continuous high speed.
- Chill the serving glass beforehand to keep the frappé thicker for longer.
