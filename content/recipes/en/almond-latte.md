# Almond Latte

A comforting latte with the nutty, sweet flavor of toasted almonds.

---

## Ingredients
- 1 shot (30ml) of Gia or Miscela Bar espresso
- 15ml Monin Almond Syrup
- 200ml steamed milk
- Almond flakes for garnish (optional)

---

## Instructions
1. Brew a single shot of Gia or Miscela Bar espresso into a cup.
2. Add the Monin Almond Syrup to the espresso and stir gently.
3. Steam the milk until it has a smooth, velvety microfoam.
4. Pour the steamed milk over the espresso mixture, finishing with a thin layer of foam.
5. Garnish with a sprinkle of almond flakes if desired.

---

## Pro-Tips
- Ensure the syrup is well mixed with the hot espresso before adding the milk to prevent it from settling at the bottom.
