# Masala Chai
A warming and aromatic Indian spiced tea made with robust black tea, milk, and a blend of fragrant spices.

---
## Ingredients
- 5g Assam Black Tea Leaves (or 2 strong black tea bags)
- 120ml Water
- 120ml Whole Milk
- 15ml Monin Chai Tea Syrup
- 1 slice Fresh Ginger (crushed)
- 2 Cardamom Pods (crushed)
- 1 small Cinnamon Stick

---
## Instructions
1. In a small saucepan, bring the water to a boil with the crushed ginger, cardamom, and cinnamon.
2. Lower the heat and add the black tea leaves. Simmer for 2 minutes.
3. Add the whole milk to the saucepan and bring the mixture back to a gentle simmer.
4. Stir in the Monin Chai Tea Syrup.
5. Strain the chai into a cup to remove the tea leaves and spices.
6. Serve hot, optionally dusted with a little ground cinnamon.

---
## Pro-Tips
- Simmering the spices first allows their essential oils to infuse the water thoroughly.
- Watch closely when boiling the milk to prevent it from spilling over the pan.
