# Espresso Tonic
A refreshing bubbly coffee drink combining the bitterness of espresso with the sweetness and fizz of tonic water.

---

## Ingredients
- 1 double shot (60 ml) Gia or Miscela Bar espresso
- 150 ml premium tonic water
- 150 g ice cubes
- 1 slice fresh lemon or lime (for garnish)

---

## Instructions
1. Fill a 250 ml glass with ice cubes.
2. Pour the tonic water over the ice.
3. Gently extract a double shot of Gia or Miscela Bar espresso.
4. Slowly pour the espresso over the back of a spoon onto the tonic water to create a layered effect.
5. Garnish with a slice of lemon or lime and serve immediately.

---

## Pro-Tips
- Pour the espresso slowly over a spoon to keep the distinct layers.
- Ensure the tonic water is ice cold before serving to preserve the carbonation.
