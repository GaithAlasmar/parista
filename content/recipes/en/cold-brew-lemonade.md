# Cold Brew Lemonade
An unexpected but highly refreshing combination of smooth cold brew and zesty lemonade.

---

## Ingredients
- 100 ml cold brew coffee concentrate
- 100 ml fresh lemonade
- 15 ml Monin Lemon Syrup or Monin Simple Syrup (depending on lemonade sweetness)
- 150 g ice cubes
- 1 lemon wedge (for garnish)
- 1 sprig fresh mint (optional)

---

## Instructions
1. Fill a glass completely with ice.
2. Pour the fresh lemonade and Monin syrup over the ice and stir gently.
3. Slowly pour the cold brew concentrate over the lemonade to create a subtle layered effect.
4. Garnish with a lemon wedge and a sprig of fresh mint.
5. Stir before drinking to combine the flavors.

---

## Pro-Tips
- Adjust the ratio of cold brew to lemonade based on the coffee's strength and roast profile.
- A floral or fruity cold brew roast profile works best to complement the citrus notes.
