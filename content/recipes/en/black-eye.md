# Black Eye
A regular drip coffee with a double espresso shot added for an intense caffeine boost.

---
## Ingredients
- 200 ml freshly brewed drip coffee
- 2 shots (60 ml) "Gia" or "Miscela Bar" espresso

---
## Instructions
1. Brew your standard drip coffee and pour it into a mug.
2. Extract a double shot of "Gia" or "Miscela Bar" espresso.
3. Pour the double espresso directly into the drip coffee.
4. Serve hot.

---
## Pro-Tips
- This drink is intensely caffeinated, so ensure the espresso shots are perfectly dialed in to avoid excess bitterness.
