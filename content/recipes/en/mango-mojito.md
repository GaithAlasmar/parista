# Mango Mojito
A tropical twist on the classic mojito, featuring sweet and juicy mango flavors.

---
## Ingredients
- 20 ml Monin Mango Purée
- 10 ml Monin Mojito Mint Syrup
- 8 fresh mint leaves
- 2 lime wedges
- 200 ml sparkling water
- 150 g ice cubes

---
## Instructions
1. Muddle the mint leaves and lime wedges gently in the glass.
2. Pour in the Monin Mango Purée and Monin Mojito Mint Syrup.
3. Add the ice cubes to fill the glass.
4. Pour the sparkling water over the ice.
5. Stir vigorously to dissolve the purée evenly into the drink.
6. Garnish with a fresh mint sprig and a slice of lime or mango.

---
## Pro-Tips
- Since purées are thicker than syrups, make sure to stir thoroughly so the flavor is consistent from the first sip to the last.
- A dash of fresh lime juice can balance the sweetness of the mango purée perfectly.
