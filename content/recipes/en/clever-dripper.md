# Clever Dripper
A hybrid brewer combining the rich extraction of full-immersion with the clean cup of a paper-filtered pour-over.

---

## Ingredients
- 20g of medium-coarse ground coffee
- 300ml of hot water (92-96°C)
- 1:15 coffee to water ratio

---

## Instructions
1. Place a paper filter in the Clever Dripper and rinse it thoroughly with hot water to remove any paper taste and preheat the brewer. Drain the water.
2. Add the 20g of ground coffee into the filter and shake gently to level it.
3. Start a timer and pour 300ml of hot water evenly over the grounds, ensuring all coffee is saturated.
4. Give it a gentle stir to break any clumps.
5. Cover the dripper with its lid and let it steep for 2 minutes and 30 seconds.
6. At the 2:30 mark, give the crust one final gentle stir.
7. Place the Clever Dripper on top of your mug or carafe to activate the drawdown valve.
8. The drawdown should take about 45 to 60 seconds. Wait until all coffee has drained.
9. Remove the dripper and enjoy your brew.

---

## Pro-Tips
- Try adjusting the steep time; a longer steep can increase body, while a shorter steep will highlight acidity.
- Pouring the water first and then adding coffee (water-first method) can lead to a faster drawdown and a cleaner cup.
