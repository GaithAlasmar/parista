# Caramel Milkshake

Buttery, golden, and irresistibly sweet — **3 scoops vanilla ice cream** blended with milk, **Monin Caramel Sauce** stirred into the blender and drizzled along the cup wall for a double caramel hit. Built in the **Sonifer blender** for a consistently thick, smooth texture.

---

## Ingredients

- 3 large scoops vanilla ice cream (~180 g)
- 80 ml whole milk *(splash — just enough to blend)*
- 25 ml **Monin Caramel Sauce** *(in the blender)*
- 10 ml **Monin Caramel Sauce** *(cup wall drizzle)*
- Whipped cream — for topping
- Extra **Monin Caramel Sauce** — drizzle for topping

---

## Method

1. **Cup wall caramel.** Drizzle **10 ml Monin Caramel Sauce** in a spiral along the inside of the cup before pouring — this creates the signature golden swirl visible through the cup.
2. **Load the Sonifer blender:**
   - 80 ml whole milk *(first — protects blades)*
   - 25 ml **Monin Caramel Sauce**
   - 3 scoops vanilla ice cream *(last)*
3. **Blend on medium-high** for **20–30 seconds** until smooth and golden. Do not over-blend — this thins the texture.
4. **Consistency check:** Should be thick and slow-pouring. Too thick → add 15 ml milk and pulse 5 s.
5. **Pour into the caramel-lined cup.**
6. **Top with whipped cream.**
7. Finish with a final drizzle of **Monin Caramel Sauce** over the cream.

---

## Key Rules for Milkshakes

> ⚠️ **No ice.** Ice cream is the chilling agent. Ice dilutes the milkshake and destroys the thick, creamy texture.

- The cup wall drizzle in step 1 is what makes this drink look premium — never skip it.
- Serve immediately — milkshakes separate quickly.
