# Flavoured Latte

A customisable iced latte built on **Gia espresso** and your choice of **Monin** syrup — Hazelnut, Vanilla, or Caramel. The cleanest way to explore the Monin flavour range with a coffee backbone.

---

## Ingredients

- 250 ml ice
- 150 ml whole milk
- 2 shots (60 ml) double espresso — **Gia beans**
- **20 ml Monin Syrup** — choose one:
  - 🟤 **Monin Hazelnut Syrup**
  - 🟡 **Monin Vanilla Syrup**
  - 🟠 **Monin Caramel Syrup**

---

## Method

1. **Pull the espresso.** Extract a double shot from **Gia beans**. Set aside to cool slightly.
2. **Syrup base.** Pour **20 ml of your chosen Monin syrup** into the bottom of the cup.
3. **Fill with ice.** Pack the cup with ice cubes.
4. **Add the milk.** Pour **150 ml whole milk** over the ice.
5. **Float the espresso.** Slowly pour the double espresso over the back of a spoon to layer it on top.
6. **Serve as-is** for layered presentation, or stir gently before handing to the customer.

---

## Syrup Character Guide

| Monin Syrup | Flavour Profile | Pairs Best With |
|---|---|---|
| Hazelnut | Warm, toasty, nutty | Full-bodied Gia beans |
| Vanilla | Sweet, creamy, soft | Lighter Miscela Bar beans |
| Caramel | Buttery, rich, golden | Either bean |

---

## Tips

- Use **30 ml** of syrup for a sweeter drink (customer preference).
- Combine **Hazelnut + Vanilla** (10 ml each) for a popular "Nutty Vanilla Latte" off-menu option.
- For a **hot version**: steam milk to 60–65 °C, stir syrup in at the base, and pour in espresso last.
