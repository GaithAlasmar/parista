# Vanilla Latte

The most popular flavoured latte in the world. A **double espresso and steamed milk** enriched with **15ml of Monin Vanilla Syrup** — naturally sweet, lightly fragrant, and endlessly approachable. The perfect introduction to flavoured coffee.

---

## Ingredients

- **30ml** single espresso (9g) — **Gia beans** or **Miscela Bar**
- **15ml** Monin Vanilla Syrup
- **180ml** whole milk, steamed to silky microfoam

---

## Instructions

1. **Add syrup first.** Pour **15ml of Monin Vanilla Syrup** into the bottom of a pre-warmed cup or glass.
2. **Pull the espresso.** Extract **9g** of coffee and pour directly over the syrup. Stir briefly to combine — the espresso will dissolve the syrup instantly.
3. **Steam the milk.** Steam **220ml** of whole milk to **60–65°C** with smooth, velvety microfoam.
4. **Pour and art.** Pour the steamed milk into the espresso-syrup base. Finish with a latte art pattern (heart or tulip).
5. Serve immediately.

---

## Pro-Tips

- **Syrup amount is adjustable:** 15ml is the standard. For a sweeter drink, increase to 20ml. For a lightly sweet hint, use 10ml.
- The **Monin Vanilla** is a clean, round vanilla flavour — not synthetic. It complements both Gia and Miscela Bar beans.
- For an **Iced Vanilla Latte:** add syrup to the bottom, fill with ice, add cold milk, float the espresso on top.
- Combine **Vanilla + Hazelnut** (10ml each) for a "Nutella Latte" effect — off-menu favourite.
