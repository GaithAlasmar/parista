# Strawberry Milkshake

Bright, fruity, and creamy — **3 scoops vanilla ice cream** blended with milk and **Monin Strawberry Syrup** in the **Sonifer blender**. The syrup gives a vivid pink colour and an authentic sweet strawberry flavour without any artificial iciness.

---

## Ingredients

- 3 large scoops vanilla ice cream (~180 g)
- 80 ml whole milk *(splash — just enough to blend)*
- 25 ml **Monin Strawberry Syrup**
- Whipped cream — for topping
- Fresh strawberry or strawberry-coloured sprinkles — optional garnish

---

## Method

1. **Load the Sonifer blender:**
   - 80 ml whole milk *(first — protects blades)*
   - 25 ml **Monin Strawberry Syrup**
   - 3 scoops vanilla ice cream *(last)*
2. **Blend on medium-high** for **20–30 seconds** until evenly pink and smooth. Do not over-blend.
3. **Consistency check:** Thick and slow-pouring. Too thick → add 15 ml milk and pulse 5 s.
4. **Pour into cup.**
5. **Top with whipped cream** in a generous mound.
6. Optional: garnish with a strawberry slice on the rim or pink sprinkles.

---

## Key Rules for Milkshakes

> ⚠️ **No ice.** Ice cream keeps it cold and thick — ice would dilute the creaminess and turn it watery.

- For a **stronger strawberry flavour**, increase Monin Strawberry Syrup to 30 ml.
- Serve immediately — milkshakes begin to separate after 5 minutes.
