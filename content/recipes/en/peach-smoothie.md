# Peach Smoothie

A light, refreshing slush built entirely from ice and **Monin Peach Syrup** — no dairy, no coffee, no blender base. Blend to a smooth slush texture in the **Sonifer blender** and serve immediately. Perfect for customers wanting something fruity and caffeine-free.

---

## Ingredients

- 1 cup ice (approx. 200 g)
- 80 ml cold water *(splash — just enough to blend)*
- 40 ml **Monin Peach Syrup**

---

## Method

1. **Load the Sonifer blender:**
   - 80 ml cold water *(first — protects blades and helps blend)*
   - 40 ml **Monin Peach Syrup**
   - 200 g ice *(last — on top)*
2. **Blend on high** for **25–35 seconds** until the mixture reaches a smooth, slushy consistency — uniform throughout with no ice chunks.
3. **Consistency check:** Should be thick like a slush, pourable but not watery. Too thick → add 20 ml water and pulse 5 s. Too thin → add more ice and re-blend.
4. **Pour into cup.** The slush should pour slowly and hold its texture.
5. Serve immediately with a straw.

---

## Tips

- This drink is **dairy-free and caffeine-free** — a great option for non-coffee and lactose-intolerant customers.
- For a **stronger peach flavour**, increase Monin Peach Syrup to 50 ml.
- Add a squeeze of **Monin Lemon Syrup** (5 ml) for a peach-lemonade slush variant.
- The ratio of syrup to water and ice is key — too much water makes it thin; too little makes it overly sweet.
