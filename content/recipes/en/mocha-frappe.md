# Mocha Frappe

A blended coffee-chocolate powerhouse — **Gia espresso**, frappe base, dark chocolate sauce, and milk, all crushed smooth in the **Sonifer blender**. Thick, cold, and intensely satisfying.

---

## Ingredients

- 250 g ice (crushed or cubed)
- 130 ml whole milk
- 60 ml double espresso — **Gia beans** (cooled)
- 30 ml frappe base powder (dissolved in 30 ml cold water)
- 25 ml **Monin Dark Chocolate Sauce**
- Whipped cream — for topping
- **Monin Dark Chocolate Sauce** — extra drizzle for topping

---

## Method

1. **Pull and cool the espresso.** Extract a double shot from **Gia beans**. Pour over a small handful of ice in a separate cup to flash-chill. Do not use hot espresso in the blender.
2. **Prepare frappe base.** Dissolve **30 ml frappe base powder** in **30 ml cold water** and stir until smooth.
3. **Load the Sonifer blender** in this order:
   - 130 ml whole milk *(first — protects blades)*
   - 25 ml **Monin Dark Chocolate Sauce**
   - Dissolved frappe base
   - 60 ml cooled espresso
   - 250 g ice *(last — on top)*
4. **Blend on high** for **35–45 seconds** until completely smooth with no ice chunks.
5. **Check consistency.** The frappe should coat a spoon. Adjust: too thick → add 20 ml milk; too thin → add a handful of ice and re-blend 10 seconds.
2. **Frappe Base Powder (30g - 40g)** (adds texture and prevents separation)
6. **Pour into cup.** Drizzle **Monin Dark Chocolate Sauce** along the inner cup wall first.
7. **Top with whipped cream** and a crosshatch drizzle of dark chocolate sauce.
8. Cap with a dome lid and serve immediately.

---

## Tips

- Dissolving the frappe base before blending prevents clumping and gives a smoother, more consistent texture.
- For a **double-chocolate** version: replace the milk with chocolate milk or add 10 ml extra chocolate sauce.
- Build to order only — do not pre-blend.
