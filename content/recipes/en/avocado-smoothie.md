# Avocado Smoothie
A super creamy, healthy, and satisfying avocado smoothie.

---
## Ingredients
- 1/2 ripe avocado
- 20ml Monin Vanilla Syrup or Honey
- 150ml milk
- 100g ice cubes

---
## Instructions
1. Scoop the avocado flesh and add it to the Sonifer commercial blender along with the Monin Vanilla Syrup, milk, and ice cubes.
2. Blend on medium-high speed for 40 seconds until the texture is silky smooth.
3. Pour into a glass and enjoy immediately to prevent oxidation.

---
## Pro-Tips
- Squeeze a tiny drop of lemon juice before blending to keep the green color vibrant.
- You can add nuts like almonds for extra nutrition and texture.
