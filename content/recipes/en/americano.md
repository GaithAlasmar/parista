# Iced Americano

The purest expression of **Gia beans** on the menu — two shots of espresso, cold water, and ice. No milk, no sugar, no distractions. Fast to build and endlessly refreshing.

---

## Ingredients

- 250 ml ice
- 150 ml cold water
- 2 shots (60 ml) double espresso — **Gia beans**

---

## Method

1. **Pull the espresso.** Extract a double shot from freshly calibrated **Gia beans** on the Casadio Nettuno. Set aside.
2. **Fill the cup with ice.** Use a large cup packed with ice cubes.
3. **Add cold water.** Pour **150 ml cold water** over the ice.
4. **Pour the espresso.** Add the double shot directly over the water and ice — no need to float or layer.
5. Serve immediately. No straw needed.

---

## Tips

- **Water-to-espresso ratio** is key: 150 ml water to 60 ml espresso gives a 1:2.5 dilution — strong and clean. Adjust to customer preference (up to 200 ml water for a lighter drink).
- The **crema** from the espresso will float briefly on top — this is normal and desirable.
- For customers wanting a **hot Americano**: replace ice and cold water with 150 ml hot water. Add espresso last to preserve the crema.
- **Miscela Bar** beans give a notably smooth, low-bitterness Americano — great recommendation for customers who find standard Americanos too harsh.
