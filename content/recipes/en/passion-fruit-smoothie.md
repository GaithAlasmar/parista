# Passion Fruit Smoothie

A vibrant, tropical slush built on ice and **Monin Passion Fruit Syrup** — blended to a thick, smooth consistency in the **Sonifer blender**. The passion fruit syrup delivers a bright, tangy-sweet flavour that's intensely refreshing. No dairy, no coffee.

---

## Ingredients

- 1 cup ice (approx. 200 g)
- 80 ml cold water *(splash — just enough to blend)*
- 40 ml **Monin Passion Fruit Syrup**

---

## Method

1. **Load the Sonifer blender:**
   - 80 ml cold water *(first — protects blades)*
   - 40 ml **Monin Passion Fruit Syrup**
   - 200 g ice *(last — on top)*
2. **Blend on high** for **25–35 seconds** until a uniform slush forms — no ice chunks visible.
3. **Consistency check:** Thick and slushy, pours slowly. Too thick → add 20 ml water and pulse. Too thin → add more ice and re-blend.
4. **Pour into cup** and serve immediately with a straw.

---

## Tips

- Passion fruit has a naturally tangy, slightly sour flavour — for a sweeter result, add 10 ml **Monin Mango Syrup** alongside the passion fruit.
- This pairs beautifully with the **Blue Ocean Mojito** for a dual-colour layered drink (advanced technique — pour gently over the back of a spoon to layer).
- Dairy-free and caffeine-free. Great for health-conscious customers.
