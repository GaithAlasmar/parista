# Nutella Mocha

A decadent mocha blending rich espresso with creamy hazelnut chocolate flavors.

---

## Ingredients
- 1 shot (30ml) of Gia or Miscela Bar espresso
- 15ml Monin Dark Chocolate Sauce
- 10ml Monin Hazelnut Syrup
- 200ml steamed milk
- Crushed hazelnuts for garnish

---

## Instructions
1. Brew a single shot of Gia or Miscela Bar espresso into a cup.
2. Add the Monin Dark Chocolate Sauce and Monin Hazelnut Syrup to recreate the Nutella flavor.
3. Stir thoroughly until the sauce is completely integrated with the espresso.
4. Steam the milk until you achieve a silky, smooth microfoam.
5. Pour the steamed milk over the espresso and chocolate hazelnut mixture.
6. Garnish with crushed roasted hazelnuts for extra crunch.

---

## Pro-Tips
- Using the combination of Monin Dark Chocolate Sauce and Hazelnut Syrup allows you to achieve a consistent Nutella flavor that dissolves much easier than actual Nutella spread.
