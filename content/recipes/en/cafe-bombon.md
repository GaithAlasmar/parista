# Café Bombón
A visually stunning layered drink originating from Spain, using espresso and sweetened condensed milk.

---
## Ingredients
- 1 shot (30 ml) "Gia" or "Miscela Bar" espresso
- 30 ml sweetened condensed milk
- (Optional) 5 ml Monin Vanilla syrup for an aromatic touch

---
## Instructions
1. Pour the sweetened condensed milk into a clear espresso glass.
2. Carefully extract a single shot of "Gia" or "Miscela Bar" espresso over the back of a spoon to layer it above the condensed milk.
3. Add a dash of Monin Vanilla syrup if desired.
4. Serve with a small spoon so the guest can stir the layers together before drinking.

---
## Pro-Tips
- Pouring the espresso slowly over a spoon is crucial for creating the striking two-toned visual effect.
