# Strawberry Mojito

A best-selling mocktail that blends the classic refreshing mint and lime profile with the sweet, fruity burst of Monin Strawberry Syrup. 

---

## Ingredients

- **20 ml** Monin Strawberry Syrup
- **1/2** fresh lime, cut into wedges
- **8-10** fresh mint leaves
- **150 ml** soda water (club soda or Sprite/7Up)
- **200 g** crushed ice
- Fresh strawberry slice for garnish

---

## Instructions

1. **Muddle:** Place the lime wedges and fresh mint leaves into the bottom of a sturdy highball glass. Gently muddle to release the lime juice and mint oils. (Do not crush the mint into pieces, just press it).
2. **Add syrup:** Pour 20 ml of Monin Strawberry Syrup over the muddled lime and mint.
3. **Add ice:** Fill the glass to the top with crushed ice.
4. **Top with soda:** Pour soda water over the ice until the glass is full.
5. **Stir:** Using a long bar spoon, gently stir from the bottom up to mix the strawberry syrup and mint leaves throughout the drink.
6. **Garnish:** Garnish with a fresh strawberry slice and an extra sprig of mint.

---

## Pro-Tips

- **Gentle muddling:** Mint leaves contain bitter chlorophyll. Press them gently to release oils; over-muddling or shredding them will make the drink taste bitter.
- **Crushed ice is key:** Crushed ice cools the drink faster and provides the classic, dense mojito texture. Cubed ice will not hold the ingredients in suspension properly.
- **Soda choice:** Club soda yields a sharper, less sweet drink. Using Sprite or 7Up makes it sweeter and more crowd-pleasing—adjust the Monin syrup slightly if using sweetened soda.
