# Matcha Frappé

An earthy, sweet, and refreshing blended green tea beverage.

---

## Ingredients
- 1.5 tsp (approx. 4-5 g) premium matcha powder
- 20 ml Monin Vanilla Syrup (or Monin Classic Sweetener)
- 120 ml whole milk
- 1 scoop (20 g) vanilla frappe base powder
- 200 g ice cubes
- Whipped cream (for garnish)
- A pinch of matcha powder (for garnish)

---

## Instructions
1. In a small cup, whisk the matcha powder with 15 ml of warm water to create a smooth paste, ensuring there are no lumps.
2. Pour the matcha paste into the Sonifer commercial blender.
3. Add the whole milk, Monin Vanilla Syrup, vanilla frappe base powder, and ice cubes.
4. Blend in the Sonifer commercial blender on high for 30-45 seconds until smooth and frosty.
5. Pour the blended drink into a tall glass.
6. Top with a swirl of whipped cream and lightly dust with matcha powder.

---

## Pro-Tips
- Whisking the matcha before blending prevents dry green tea lumps in the final drink.
- Adjust the amount of Monin Vanilla Syrup depending on the natural sweetness of your frappe base.
