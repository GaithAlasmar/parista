# White Hot Chocolate

Sweeter and creamier than its dark counterpart — **30g of white chocolate sauce dissolved into 250ml of steamed whole milk**. The result is a naturally sweet, vanilla-tinted, dessert-like hot drink. Beloved by customers who prefer a milder, sweeter alternative to regular hot chocolate.

---

## Ingredients

- **250ml** whole milk
- **30g** Monin White Chocolate Sauce
- Optional toppings: whipped cream, white chocolate shavings, sprinkles

---

## Instructions

1. **White chocolate base.** Squeeze **30g of Monin White Chocolate Sauce** into the bottom of a pre-warmed mug.
2. **Steam the milk.** Steam **280ml** of whole milk to **60–65°C** (or **50–55°C for children**). Create smooth, velvety foam — the richer the foam, the more dessert-like the experience.
3. **Pour and stir.** Pour steamed milk into the white chocolate sauce. Stir thoroughly with a long spoon from the bottom — white chocolate sauce can cling to the sides of the cup.
4. **Top with garnish.** Whipped cream and white chocolate shavings on top create a premium, dessert-worthy presentation.
5. Serve immediately.

---

## Pro-Tips

- **White chocolate sauce is naturally sweeter** than dark chocolate sauce — start with 25g for customers who prefer it less sweet, and go up to 35g for those with a very sweet tooth.
- For a **White Hot Chocolate Mocha**, split the sauce 15g white + 15g dark chocolate — this creates a "marble" flavour effect.
- Add **Monin Vanilla Syrup (10ml)** alongside the white chocolate sauce for an even more complex, fragrant flavour.
- **Children's temperature: 50–55°C** — always.
