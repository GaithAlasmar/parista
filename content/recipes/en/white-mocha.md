# White Mocha

Decadent and creamy, the White Mocha replaces dark chocolate with **25g of Monin White Chocolate Sauce** — producing a sweeter, creamier, more dessert-like coffee experience. This is also the signature base of the **Spanish Latte**.

---

## Ingredients

- **30ml** single espresso (9g) — **Gia beans**
- **25g** Monin White Chocolate Sauce
- **180ml** whole milk, steamed to silky microfoam

---

## Instructions

1. **Sauce base.** Squeeze **25g of Monin White Chocolate Sauce** into the bottom of a pre-warmed cup. Swirl to coat the bottom.
2. **Pull the espresso.** Extract **9g** and pour directly over the sauce. Stir to integrate — the espresso will help melt the sauce.
3. **Steam the milk.** Steam **220ml** of whole milk to **60–65°C** with smooth, velvety microfoam.
4. **Pour.** Pour steamed milk into the white chocolate-espresso base. Create latte art as desired.
5. Serve immediately.

---

## Pro-Tips

- **Sauce vs. Syrup:** White chocolate sauce is thicker and creamier than white chocolate syrup — always use sauce for this recipe. The sauce version has a richer, more authentic white chocolate flavour.
- For a **White Mocha Frappé:** blend white chocolate sauce + cold milk + espresso + ice until smooth.
- Adding **Monin Vanilla Syrup (10ml)** alongside the white chocolate sauce creates a sweeter, more layered flavour (this is essentially the Spanish Latte base).
- The white chocolate can overpower delicate espresso — use **Gia beans** for their boldness.
