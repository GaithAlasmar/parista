# Orange Espresso
A bright and citrusy coffee beverage matching freshly squeezed orange juice with bold espresso.

---

## Ingredients
- 1 double shot (60 ml) Gia or Miscela Bar espresso
- 120 ml freshly squeezed orange juice
- 10 ml Monin Vanilla Syrup (optional, for a touch of sweetness)
- 150 g ice cubes
- 1 orange peel (for garnish)

---

## Instructions
1. Fill a glass with ice cubes.
2. Add the Monin Vanilla Syrup and freshly squeezed orange juice.
3. Extract a double shot of Gia or Miscela Bar espresso.
4. Carefully pour the hot espresso over the ice and orange juice layer.
5. Express the oils from the orange peel over the drink and drop it in as a garnish.

---

## Pro-Tips
- Strain the orange juice to remove excess pulp for a smoother texture.
- Shake the orange juice with ice before assembling for a colder, more refreshing drink.
