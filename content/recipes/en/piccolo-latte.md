# Piccolo Latte
A petite milk drink made with a ristretto shot, delivering a sweet, milk-forward espresso flavor.

---
## Ingredients
- 1 shot (20 ml) "Gia" or "Miscela Bar" ristretto
- 80 ml steamed milk

---
## Instructions
1. Extract a single ristretto shot (20 ml) of "Gia" or "Miscela Bar" into a small 100 ml demitasse or glass.
2. Steam the milk to create a smooth, velvety microfoam suitable for latte art.
3. Pour the steamed milk over the ristretto, finishing with a small piece of latte art if desired.
4. Serve warm.

---
## Pro-Tips
- The ristretto shot highlights the sweeter, less bitter notes of the espresso, which harmonizes perfectly with the small amount of milk.
