# Coconut Cold Brew
A smooth and tropical cold brew coffee elevated with creamy coconut milk and coconut syrup.

---

## Ingredients
- 150 ml cold brew coffee concentrate
- 15 ml Monin Coconut Syrup
- 50 ml coconut milk
- 150 g ice cubes
- Toasted coconut flakes (for garnish)

---

## Instructions
1. Fill a tall glass with ice cubes.
2. Add the Monin Coconut Syrup to the glass.
3. Pour the cold brew coffee over the ice and stir gently to combine with the syrup.
4. Top the drink with cold coconut milk to create a beautiful cascading effect.
5. Sprinkle toasted coconut flakes on top and serve.

---

## Pro-Tips
- For a richer texture, froth the coconut milk slightly before pouring.
- Adjust the Monin Coconut Syrup according to the desired sweetness.
