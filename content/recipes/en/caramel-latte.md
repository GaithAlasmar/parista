# Caramel Latte

The crowd-pleasing caramel latte — **bold espresso softened by steamed milk and enriched with 15ml of Monin Caramel Syrup**. The caramel adds a warm, buttery sweetness that pairs beautifully with the roasted notes of the espresso.

---

## Ingredients

- **30ml** single espresso (9g) — **Gia beans**
- **15ml** Monin Caramel Syrup
- **180ml** whole milk, steamed to silky microfoam
- Optional: caramel drizzle on top for presentation

---

## Instructions

1. **Caramel base.** Add **15ml Monin Caramel Syrup** to the bottom of a pre-warmed cup.
2. **Pull the espresso.** Extract **9g** and pour directly over the syrup. Stir to combine.
3. **Steam the milk.** Steam **220ml** of whole milk to **60–65°C** with smooth microfoam.
4. **Pour.** Pour the steamed milk into the espresso-caramel base. Create a latte art heart or tulip.
5. **Optional drizzle.** Use a squeeze bottle to drizzle a small spiral of caramel sauce on top for presentation.
6. Serve immediately.

---

## Pro-Tips

- **Caramel Syrup vs. Caramel Sauce:** the Monin Caramel Syrup blends smoothly into the drink; Caramel Sauce is thicker and better used as a garnish drizzle.
- For **Salted Caramel Latte:** use Monin Salted Caramel Syrup (or add a very small pinch of fleur de sel on top of the foam).
- For an **Iced Caramel Latte:** syrup → ice → cold milk → espresso float → optional caramel drizzle.
- **Gia beans'** bold, dark roast profile cuts through the sweetness perfectly — this pairing is intentional.
