# Salted Caramel Mocha

A perfect balance of sweet chocolate, savory sea salt, and rich espresso.

---

## Ingredients
- 1 shot (30ml) of Gia or Miscela Bar espresso
- 15ml Monin Dark Chocolate Sauce
- 15ml Monin Salted Caramel Syrup
- 200ml steamed milk
- Caramel drizzle for garnish
- A pinch of sea salt (optional)

---

## Instructions
1. Brew a single shot of Gia or Miscela Bar espresso into a cup.
2. Combine the Monin Dark Chocolate Sauce and Monin Salted Caramel Syrup with the hot espresso.
3. Stir well to ensure the sauce and syrup are completely melted and mixed.
4. Steam the milk to create a velvety microfoam.
5. Pour the hot milk over the sweet espresso base.
6. Garnish with a drizzle of caramel and a small pinch of sea salt.

---

## Pro-Tips
- A tiny pinch of real sea salt on top elevates the caramel flavor and adds a nice textural crunch.
