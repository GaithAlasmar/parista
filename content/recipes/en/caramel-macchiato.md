# Caramel Macchiato

A beautifully layered iced drink: **Monin Vanilla Syrup** at the base, cold milk in the middle, and **Gia espresso floated on top** — finished with a generous drizzle of **Monin Caramel Sauce** on the inner cup wall and over the top. Every sip delivers a different layer.

---

## Ingredients

- 250 ml ice
- 150 ml whole milk
- 2 shots (60 ml) double espresso — **Gia beans** (floated on top)
- 20 ml **Monin Vanilla Syrup**
- 15 ml **Monin Caramel Sauce** (for cup wall + topping)

---

## Method

1. **Pull the espresso.** Extract a double shot from **Gia beans**. Set aside — it will be added last.
2. **Caramel wall.** Drizzle **10 ml Monin Caramel Sauce** in a spiral along the inside wall of the cup before adding anything — this creates the visible caramel swirl.
3. **Vanilla base.** Pour **20 ml Monin Vanilla Syrup** into the bottom of the cup.
4. **Fill with ice.** Pack the cup generously with ice cubes.
5. **Add the milk.** Pour **150 ml whole milk** over the ice — it will naturally blend with the vanilla syrup below.
6. **Float the espresso.** Slowly pour the double espresso over the back of a spoon so it sits as a dark layer on top of the milk.
7. **Caramel drizzle.** Finish with the remaining **5 ml Monin Caramel Sauce** drizzled in a crosshatch over the espresso layer.
8. **Serve immediately** — do not stir before presenting.

---

## Tips

- The visual "macchiato" layer effect depends on pouring the espresso slowly — don't rush step 6.
- For customers who prefer it mixed: give it a gentle stir at the table.
- The **Monin Caramel Sauce** on the cup wall is what differentiates this from a vanilla latte — don't skip it.
