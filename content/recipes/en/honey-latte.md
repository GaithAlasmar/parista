# Honey Latte

A smooth and sweet latte naturally flavored with honey.

---

## Ingredients
- 1 shot (30ml) of Gia or Miscela Bar espresso
- 15ml Monin Honey Syrup (or 15g natural honey)
- 200ml steamed milk
- A pinch of cinnamon for garnish

---

## Instructions
1. Brew a single shot of Gia or Miscela Bar espresso into a cup.
2. Add the Monin Honey Syrup to the hot espresso and mix until fully dissolved.
3. Steam the milk to create a rich and creamy microfoam.
4. Pour the steamed milk over the espresso, creating latte art if desired.
5. Garnish with a light dusting of cinnamon on top.

---

## Pro-Tips
- If using raw honey instead of Monin syrup, make sure the espresso is very hot to melt it completely.
